# Harvest 2026-07-15

## Highlights
- **DC Council unanimously passes the RESALE Act.** Caps resale at 10% above face value, bans speculative ticketing and "surveillance pricing," and requires licensing/bonding for resellers of 50+ tickets/year — but a last-minute amendment stripped the mayor's authority to regulate primary-market fees and dynamic pricing, leaving primary sellers untouched. Heads to Mayor Bowser; effective January 1, 2027 if signed. [[articles/2026-07-15-washington-d-c-passes-industry-backed-price-cap-bill-stripping-primary-oversight|Washington D.C. Passes Industry-Backed Price Cap Bill, Stripping Primary Oversight in Last-Minute Amendment]]
- **German court orders FIFA to halt deceptive World Cup ticketing practices.** Frankfurt regional court's July 13 preliminary injunction bars bait-and-switch "from" pricing, hidden total cost until after seat selection, and coercive countdown timers, and applies EU Digital Services Act seller-disclosure rules to FIFA's own resale marketplace — Germany-only and not yet final, but a precedent for treating organizer-run platforms like third-party marketplaces. [[articles/2026-07-15-german-court-orders-fifa-to-halt-deceptive-world-cup-ticketing-practices|German Court Orders FIFA to Halt Deceptive World Cup Ticketing Practices]]
- **New Jersey launches a statewide "junk fees" initiative.** AG enforcement statement (drip pricing, dark patterns, consent manipulation) plus Governor Sherrill's Executive Order 19, directing agencies to recommend junk-fee rules/legislation by September 2026; penalties up to $10K/$20K per violation under the NJ Consumer Fraud Act. [[articles/2026-07-15-new-jersey-signals-focus-on-junk-fees-regulation-and-enforcement|New Jersey Signals Focus on 'Junk Fees' Regulation and Enforcement]]
- **UK Heritage Live collapses entirely, fueling a CMA-investigation push against Live Nation.** All 13 dates of its 2026 summer series cancelled with 10 days' notice; a House of Commons committee report cited Live Nation's 58–66% UK primary-ticket market share and recommended a full competition probe by end of 2026. [[articles/2026-07-15-uk-s-heritage-live-cancels-entire-2026-summer-series-amid-financial-collapse|UK's Heritage Live Cancels Entire 2026 Summer Series Amid Financial Collapse]]
- **Opinion: resale-regulation rules are outrunning platforms' ability to enforce them.** UK draft Ticket Tout Ban Bill (unlikely law before 2027-28), Ontario's CA$3K–250K enforcement blitz, California's AB 1720 (in Appropriations), and an EU Digital Fairness Act draft expected Q3 2026 are all live — while World Cup dynamic pricing still rose primary prices ~35% on average. [[articles/2026-07-15-new-resale-rules-are-coming-someone-has-to-apply-them|New resale rules are coming. Someone has to apply them]]
