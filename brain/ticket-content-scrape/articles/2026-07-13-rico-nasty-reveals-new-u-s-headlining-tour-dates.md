# Rico Nasty Reveals New U.S. Headlining Tour Dates

## Summary

Rico Nasty announced a new U.S. headlining tour running August 30 (St. Louis) through October 16 (San Antonio), running alongside her previously announced supporting-act dates with Hayley Williams. Tickets follow an artist presale starting July 8 and general on-sale July 10, timed to coincide with her upcoming album *RX*, out July 24.

### Key Points
- New headlining dates: August 30 (St. Louis) through October 16 (San Antonio).
- Runs concurrently with her support slot on Hayley Williams' tour.
- Artist presale began July 8; general on-sale July 10.
- Coincides with her album *RX*, releasing July 24.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Rico-Nasty.webp)

Rico Nasty has announced a new headlining U.S. tour that will kick off later this summer, adding a series of solo performances to her 2026 touring schedule.

The newly revealed run begins on August 30 in St. Louis, Missouri at Red Flag. From there, the run is scheduled to make stops in cities such as Jacksonville, Saint Petersburg, Buffalo, Grand Rapids and Little Rock before wrapping up on October 16 in San Antonio, Texas at Paper Tiger.

**| RELATED:**[**Hayley Williams Reveals ‘The Hayley Williams Show’ with North, Latin American Dates**](https://www.ticketnews.com/2026/05/hayley-williams-reveals-the-hayley-williams-show/) **|**

The headlining dates will take place alongside Rico Nasty’s previously announced tour with Hayley Williams, where she is serving as the supporting act. Stops on Williams’ tour include cities such as West Palm Beach, Charleston, Raleigh, Forest Hills and Los Angeles.
