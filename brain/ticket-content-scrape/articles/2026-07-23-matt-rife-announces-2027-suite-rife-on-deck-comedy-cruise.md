# Matt Rife Announces 2027 “Suite Rife On Deck” Comedy Cruise

## Summary

Comedian Matt Rife has announced "Suite Rife On Deck," a four-night comedy cruise sailing from Miami to Grand Turk in 2027, produced with Cloud 9 Adventures and 100x Hospitality. The cruise features a large lineup of guest comedians alongside live music and DJ sets, plus themed programming including a "Welcome Roast Toast." Registration is open now, with general cabin sales opening July 28.

### Key Points
- Four-night cruise departs Miami with a port stop in Grand Turk, sailing in 2027.
- Lineup includes 17+ guest comedians (Craig Robinson, Nate Jackson, Erik Griffin, Finesse Mitchell and others), plus musician Blake Whiten and three DJs.
- Produced by 100x Hospitality (CEO Dan Berkowitz) in partnership with Cloud 9 Adventures.
- General public cabin sales open July 28 at 1 p.m. Eastern via suiterifeondeck.com.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Matt-Rife-Suite-Rife-on-Deck.webp)

Comedian Matt Rife has announced “Suite Rife On Deck,” a four-night comedy cruise set to sail in 2027.

The cruise will depart from Miami and include a port stop in Grand Turk. During the sailing, guests will have access to comedy performances, themed events and live entertainment featuring Rife and a lineup of guest comedians.

Joining Rife on the cruise are Craig Robinson, Nate Jackson, Lukas Zelnick, Erik Griffin, Finesse Mitchell, Shapel Lacey, Ian Edwards, April Macie, Paul Elia, Martin Amini, David Lucas, Jackson McQueen, Clayton Thomas, Janet McNamara, Erica Rhodes, Cam Bertrand, Matt Lopes and Doug Key.

The event will begin with a “Welcome Roast Toast” hosted by Rife and the participating comedians. Throughout the voyage, members of the lineup will host programming and performances on deck.

In addition to the comedy lineup, Blake Whiten will perform live during the cruise. Entertainment will also include DJ sets from Cosmo Baker, DJ Rock and DJ Spider.

“Suite Rife On Deck is about creating an experience that goes far beyond a comedy show, giving fans the opportunity to spend four unforgettable days immersed in Matt’s world,” Dan Berkowitz, CEO of 100x Hospitality, said.

“We’re excited to partner with Matt and Cloud 9 Adventures to bring people together through comedy, connection, and a truly unique vacation experience.”

Registration for the cruise is now open. Cabins for “Suite Rife On Deck” will go on sale to the general public at 1 p.m. Eastern on July 28.
