# Thundercat Announces ‘Distracted AF’ Tour In Support Of New Album

## Summary

Thundercat has announced a 32-date North American headlining tour, the "Distracted AF Tour," running from October 2 to December 11, in support of his latest album of the same name. Support acts include Channel Tres, TiaCorine, Fera, 54 Ultra and Kitty Ca$h, with tickets going on sale the following Friday.

### Key Points
- Thundercat's "Distracted AF Tour" kicks off Oct. 2 in Miami and concludes Dec. 11 in Austin.
- 32 dates across the US and Canada, with rotating support acts.
- Tour supports his album Distracted AF, released April 3 on Brainfeeder Records, featuring guests like Tame Impala and A$AP Rocky.
- Tickets go on sale Friday at 10 a.m. local time via the artist's official site.

Thundercat is preparing to hit the road once again, but this time headlining a tour of his own: the “Distracted AF Tour.” The 32-date tour will kick off on Oct. 2 in Miami, FL, at the Miami Beach Bandshell.

Thundercat will take the new trek, supporting his latest album, *Distracted AF*, around the United States, hitting cities like Tampa, FL, Portland, OR, Buffalo, NY, Houston, TX, and more, before concluding on Dec. 11 at the Holiday Hootenanny in Austin, TX. Joining him on the road are Channel Tres, TiaCorine, Fera, 54 Ultra, and Kitty Ca$h.

Thundercat released his latest LP, *Distracted AF*, on April 3 on Brainfeeder Records. *Distracted AF* is his first full-length project since 2020 and features 15 tracks with notable guest appearances from Tame Impala, Mac Miller, Lil Yachty, A$AP Rocky, Channel Tres, and Willow Smith.

Tickets go on sale on Friday at 10 a.m. local time. Fans can grab tickets [here](https://theamazingthundercat.com/tour).
