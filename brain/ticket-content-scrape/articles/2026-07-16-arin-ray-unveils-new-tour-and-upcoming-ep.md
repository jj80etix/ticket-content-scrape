# Arin Ray Unveils New Tour And Upcoming EP

## Summary

R&B singer Arin Ray announced an eight-city "Limbo Tour" across the US in support of his forthcoming EP, led by the single "Sweet Thang." The intimate run kicks off September 25 at Crown Hill Theatre in New York and wraps October 11 in Sacramento, with tickets on sale now via Ticketmaster.

### Key Points
- Tour opens Sept. 25 at Crown Hill Theatre, New York, NY, and closes Oct. 11 at The Rink, Sacramento, CA.
- Eight cities total: New York, Washington DC, Atlanta, Houston, Dallas, Mesa, Los Angeles, Sacramento.
- Tickets are on sale now via Ticketmaster.
- Tour supports Arin Ray's upcoming "Limbo" EP, previewed by the single "Sweet Thang."
- Cincinnati native Arin Ray plays venues including Union Stage (DC), Purgatory (Atlanta), Warehouse Live (Houston), and Echoplex (LA).

Arin Ray is having a moment with his new single, “Sweet Thang,” and he’s looking to keep his foot on the gas with a new EP and a supporting tour. Arin Ray will headline the intimate eight-city “Limbo Tour” through America, with the trek kicking off on Sept. 25 at the Crown Hill Theatre in New York, NY. From there, the Cincinnati native will hit Washington, D.C., Atlanta, GA, Houston, TX, Dallas, TX, Mesa, AZ, Los Angeles, and finish up in Sacramento, CA. Tickets are on sale [now](https://www.ticketmaster.com/arin-ray-tickets/artist/1810648).

The *Limbo* EP was teased with the first taste of the project being his latest single, “Sweet Thang.” The track is a smooth cut that blends R&B, soul, and hip-hop sensibilities with a voice that is both charming and confident. The new EP is set to “explore love, uncertainty, growth, and the emotional in-between spaces that define life’s most transformative moments,” per an official press release.

The *Limbo* EP is arriving later this summer.
