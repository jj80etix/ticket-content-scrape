# Ben Jackson Walker Returns to Broadway’s ‘& Juliet’ as Corey Mach Joins Cast

## Summary

Broadway's "& Juliet" is welcoming two new cast members in August: original cast member Ben Jackson Walker returns as Romeo, and Broadway veteran Corey Mach joins as Shakespeare. The casting news comes during the show's fourth Broadway summer, shortly after it celebrated its 1,500th performance.

### Key Points
- Ben Jackson Walker returns to the role of Romeo starting Aug. 13, replacing Liam Pearce.
- Corey Mach joins as Shakespeare starting Aug. 4, replacing Drew Gehling; Mach previously played the role on the North American tour.
- "& Juliet" opened on Broadway in November 2022 and reached its 1,500th performance on June 23, 2026.
- The show earned nine Tony nominations, including Best Musical.
- Current cast also includes Gianna Harris, Alison Luff, and alternating Lance performers Joey Fatone and Paulo Szot.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/Juliet-jpg.webp)

Broadway’s “& Juliet” will welcome two new cast members this August, with original cast member Ben Jackson Walker returning to the role of Romeo and Broadway veteran Corey Mach joining the production as Shakespeare.

Walker, who originated the role of Romeo when “& Juliet” opened on Broadway, will resume the role at the Stephen Sondheim Theatre beginning August 13. He replaces Liam Pearce, whose final performance is scheduled for August 9.

Meanwhile, Mach will join the Broadway company on August 4 as Shakespeare, succeeding Drew Gehling, who is set to give his final performance on August 2. Mach previously originated the role during the North American tour of “& Juliet” before making the move to the Broadway production.

The pair will join a cast led by Gianna Harris as Juliet, Alison Luff as Anne, Michael Iván Carrier as May, Chrissy Metz as Angélique, and Nathan Levy as François. Throughout the summer, Joey Fatone of *NSYNC* and Tony Award winner Paulo Szot are alternating in the role of Lance.

The casting announcement comes during the production’s fourth summer on Broadway. “& Juliet” opened at the Stephen Sondheim Theatre in November 2022 after premiering in London’s West End. On June 23, the musical reached another milestone by celebrating its 1,500th Broadway performance.

The musical earned nine Tony Award nominations, including Best Musical. Featuring a book by David West Read, the production reimagines Shakespeare’s classic tragedy by exploring what Juliet’s life might have looked like had she survived.
