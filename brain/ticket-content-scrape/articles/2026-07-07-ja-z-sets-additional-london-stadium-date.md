# JAŸ-Z Sets Additional London Stadium Date

## Summary

JAY-Z added an additional London stadium date at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on September 4, joining previously announced Paris and Los Angeles shows as part of his "JAY-Z 30" career-anniversary concert series. It follows his sold-out July 10-11 Yankee Stadium shows marking the anniversaries of *Reasonable Doubt* and *The Blueprint*.

### Key Points
- New Tottenham Hotspur Stadium date added for Sept 4, joining Paris (Sept 10) and LA (Oct 23) shows.
- Part of the "JAY-Z 30" series marking 30 years since *Reasonable Doubt* and 25 years since *The Blueprint*.
- Precedes sold-out Yankee Stadium shows July 10-11.
- London tickets available via presale starting July 9, general on-sale July 10 at livenation.co.uk.

JAŸ-Z is bringing yet another show to London this year to celebrate his 30-year career, with an additional stop at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on Sept. 4. This new London date joins the previously [announced performances](https://news.pollstar.com/2026/06/09/jay-z-announces-paris-la-concerts/) in both Paris and Los Angeles, taking place on Sept. 10 and Oct. 23, respectively.

The announcement arrives just days before the iconic hip-hop artist takes the stage at Yankee Stadium for a sold-out affair on July 10 & 11 to mark the 30th anniversary of his debut album, *Reasonable Doubt,* and the 25th anniversary of *The Blueprint*.

Hov kicked off the summer celebration with a show-stopping performance at The Roots Picnic in Philadelphia on May 30, where the emcee was backed by the acclaimed hip-hop band, ripping through tracks from both seminal albums and more cuts from his extensive catalog.

Tickets for the London date will be available starting Thursday, 9th July at 10 a.m. local time via presales, including early access for O2 and Virgin Media customers on Priority. The general on-sale will begin Friday, 10th July at 10 a.m. local time at [livenation.co.uk](http://livenation.co.uk/). Access to Preferred Tickets is also available to Mastercard cardholders in the UK on Friday, 10th July at 10 a.m. Visit [www.priceless.com/music](http://www.priceless.com/music) to find out more.

Remaining tickets for Paris and Los Angeles are available now at [livenation.com](http://livenation.com/).
