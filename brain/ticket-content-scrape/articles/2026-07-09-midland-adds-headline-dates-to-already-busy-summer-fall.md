# Midland Adds Headline Dates To Already-Busy Summer & Fall

## Summary

Country act Midland has added new headline tour stops in Iowa, South Dakota and Texas to an already packed summer/fall schedule that includes their own "Stages Tour," opening slots for Kacey Musgraves, and co-headline dates with Clint Black.

### Key Points
- New headline dates added in Iowa, South Dakota, and Texas.
- Existing commitments include Midland's own "Stages Tour," opening for Kacey Musgraves, and co-headlining with Clint Black.
- Represented in North America by UTA's Brett Saliba.

Grammy-nominated neo-traditional country revivalists Midland have added a handful of headline dates to a jampacked summer and fall touring schedule.

In addition to their own “Stages Tour,” supporting the band’s new album of the same name, Midland will open for Kacey Musgraves and play co-headline shows with Clint Black.

Newly added dates include stops in Iowa, South Dakota and Texas.

Midland is repped by UTA’s Brett Saliba in North America.
