# Issue 145

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's Issue 145 landing page — a table of contents for the June 2026 print/digital issue rather than a standalone article. Featured content includes the LGBTIQ+ List 2026, a profile of promoter Pino Sagliocco, a Montreux Jazz Festival 60th-anniversary retrospective, a New Zealand market report, and a Take That touring-logistics feature.

### Key Points
- Cover package: LGBTIQ+ List 2026 and the "Loud & Proud" queer-artist spotlight.
- "The Architects: Pino Sagliocco" profiles the veteran promoter's career.
- Feature on Montreux Jazz Festival's 60-year archive (7,000 UNESCO-protected performances) and its brand expansion.
- Q&A with Cuffe & Taylor co-founder Peter Taylor on building an independent promoting business.
- New Zealand market-focus report and a Take That touring-logistics feature.
- Page links out to individual articles rather than containing full article text itself.
