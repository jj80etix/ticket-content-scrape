# WWE Brings WrestleMania 43 Weekend Events to Madison Square Garden

## Summary

WWE is bringing NXT Stand & Deliver, AAA Eternal Glory, and an official WrestleMania 43 Watch Party to Madison Square Garden's Infosys Theater during WrestleMania weekend in April 2027, even though WrestleMania 43 itself will be held in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The watch party will include giant LED screens, brand activations, and a Fanatics retail/experience partnership, with additional US watch-party locations to be announced.

### Key Points
- WrestleMania 43 will be held in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia — the first time WWE's flagship event has been staged outside North America.
- MSG's Infosys Theater hosts NXT Stand & Deliver, AAA Eternal Glory, and an official WatchParty during WrestleMania weekend, April 2027.
- Additional US watch-party locations, including Cosm venues, will be announced later.
- WWE is partnering with Fanatics for interactive fan experiences and exclusive retail activations tied to the weekend.
- Part of WWE's broader partnership with Saudi Arabia's General Entertainment Authority and Riyadh Season, announced September 2025.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/wwe.jpg)

WrestleMania 43 may be heading overseas, but WWE is making sure New York City still gets a major piece of the action.

WWE announced that **NXT Stand & Deliver**, **AAA Eternal Glory** and an official **WrestleMania 43 Watch Party** will take over the Infosys Theater at Madison Square Garden during WrestleMania weekend in April 2027.

The annual Stand & Deliver event will showcase NXT’s top talent, while Eternal Glory will bring Mexico’s Lucha Libre AAA stars to one of New York’s most iconic entertainment destinations.

Infosys Theater will also be transformed into an immersive viewing destination for WrestleMania 43. Fans can expect giant LED screens, WWE brand activations, exclusive merchandise and other attractions during the official watch parties.
