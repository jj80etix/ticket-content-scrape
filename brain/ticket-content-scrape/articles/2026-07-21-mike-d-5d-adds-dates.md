# Mike D 5D Adds Dates

## Summary

Mike D 5D — the Beastie Boys' Mike D with his sons and bandmates — expanded a previously announced five-date run into a 20-stop North American and European theater tour. Tickets for the new dates go on sale July 24.

### Key Points
- Tour runs Sept. 20–Nov. 5 across North America, UK, and mainland Europe.
- Originally five headline shows plus a Portola festival slot.
- Onsale July 24 at 10 a.m. local.

Mike D 5D — the band comprised of Beastie Boy Mike D, his sons Skyler and Davis Diamond, Eddie Ruscha, Will Graefe and Milo Ruscha — has added tour dates after announcing a limited run of shows last week.

The initial announcement included five headline performances plus a slot at San Francisco’s Portola. The new slate is much more extensive: 20 stops in theaters across North America and Europe.

The new dates begin Sept. 20 at The Observatory in Santa Ana, California, kicking off a North American leg that includes LA, Chicago, Philadelphia and more before wrapping at Toronto’s History Oct. 20. The band will play Brighton, Glasgow, Manchester and London on a UK swing before crossing the Channel for European dates in Amsterdam, Lille, Cologne, Lausanne and Lyon, with the tour wrapping in Brussels Nov. 5.

Tickets go on sale July 24 at 10 a.m. local.
