# India’s first AI-powered ticketing startup thumpN raises €3.3m

## Summary

India's thumpN, billed as the country's first AI-native live-entertainment discovery and ticketing platform, raised over €3.3m (₹35.7 Cr) in seed funding. Built by former Paytm Insider executives, thumpN centers on "Shadow," a conversational AI agent that delivers personalized event recommendations across concerts, festivals, comedy and sports regardless of which platform lists the event, differentiating itself from India's promoter-owned incumbents (BookMyShow, District by Zomato, Skillbox).

### Highlights
- thumpN raised €3.3m+ seed funding; founded by ex-Paytm Insider CEO/COO team, backed by Paytm executives and artists including Arijit Singh.
- Core product is "Shadow," a conversational AI discovery agent using natural-language interaction, distinct from being tied to a single platform's listings.
- Also building organizer-facing analytics tools covering audience taste, demand, and genre trends.
- Currently in beta across six Indian cities, with plans to expand into Tier II/III markets.

### Industry Problem
Event discovery today is fragmented across social feeds, algorithms, and word of mouth, and existing major ticketing platforms are also promoters, creating a structural conflict between discovery neutrality and self-promotion — leaving patrons without a trustworthy, personalized cross-platform discovery layer.

### Proposed Solution
thumpN's answer is an AI conversational discovery agent that stays platform-agnostic and layers organizer-facing demand analytics on top. For Etix Assistant, this is a direct AI-deployment benchmark: a natural-language, personalization-driven discovery experience plus organizer demand insights is the kind of AI-context capability worth evaluating for competitive parity.

### Key Points
- thumpN raises €3.3m+ seed round; founded by former Paytm Insider leadership.
- "Shadow" AI agent offers natural-language, cross-platform personalized event discovery.
- Also developing demand/audience-insight tools for event organizers.
- Currently beta-testing in six Indian cities, targeting Tier II/III expansion.

## India’s first AI-powered ticketing startup thumpN raises €3.3m

Built by former Paytm Insider executives, the platform aims to offer users a personalised discovery pathway to concerts, festivals, and sports

![Varun Khare](https://www.iqmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Varun-Khare.png)

thumpN CEO Varun Khare

India’s “first AI-native discovery and ticketing platform”, thumpN, has secured over €3.3 million (₹35.7 Cr) in seed funding as the startup aims to offer users a personalised way to access live entertainment across the country.

Having launched earlier this week (6 July), the platform’s leaders plan to use the capital to expand the AI-powered discovery channel and expand its presence across one of the world’s fastest-growing entertainment markets.

ThumpN is being built by a team of former leaders at Paytm Insider, which was [acquired](https://www.iqmagazine.com/2024/09/india-paytm-insider-acquired-220m/) in a deal valued at around €220 million by food delivery giant Zomato in September 2024. The deal led to the creation of District by Zomato, one of India’s leading ticketing platforms today, alongside BookMyShow and Skillbox.

At the helm of ThumpN is CEO Varun Khare, who is the former COO of Paytm Insider. Additionally, Paytm Insider’s former founder/CEO, Shreyas Srinivasan, is a director and strategic advisor to ThumpN, while the company’s head of brands and marketing is Meghana Bhogle, who was formerly the head of IPs and touring at Paytm Insider.

Among its core investors is a handful of executives from Indian fintech leader Paytm, including founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma’s family office VSS Investco and Paytm CFO Madhur Deora, according to *[Inc42](https://inc42.com/buzz/exclusive-live-entertainment-discovery-platform-thumpn-nets-3-75-mn-from-vijay-shekhar-sharma-others/).* Other investors include singers Arijit Singh, Sunidhi Chauhan, and Badshah.

> “Discovery today is fragmented across social feeds, algorithms, WhatsApp groups, and word of mouth”

ThumpN’s key feature is an AI agent, named Shadow, with which users can utilise “natural conversation” to find live events in a handful of markets across the country, with the agentic solution shaping to a user’s tastes over time. Shadow combines insights from artists, promoters, venue operators, and tastemakers to deliver personalised recommendations across concerts, comedy shows, festivals, sports, and more.

“Discovery today is fragmented across social feeds, algorithms, WhatsApp groups, and word of mouth,” says CEO Khare. “The explosion of India’s live entertainment market, combined with the shift toward conversational and AI-native consumer behaviour, has created an opportunity to rethink discovery from the ground up.”

Unlike India’s other major ticketing providers that are also promoters – BookMyShow, District by Zomato, and Skillbox – Khare told [*Inc42*](https://inc42.com/buzz/exclusive-live-entertainment-discovery-platform-thumpn-nets-3-75-mn-from-vijay-shekhar-sharma-others/) that the platform will be aimed at discovery “regardless of where they’re listed, believing that earning user trust is more important than limiting recommendations to a single platform.”

Beyond discovery, the startup is also building software tools for event organisers, with plans to provide insight into audience tastes, event demand, genre trends, and consumer behaviours.

Currently in beta testing, thumpN offers events in cities including Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Delhi, Goa, Mumbai, and Pune, with plans to expand and provide better insights into Tier II and Tier III cities that have rapidly growing urban populations.
