# FTC Returns Money to Consumers Harmed by Trend Deploy’s Deceptive Marketing

## Summary

The FTC is mailing more than $672,000 in refund checks to 9,419 consumers deceived by Trend Deploy operator Frank Romero's marketing practices. This is a routine FTC consumer-redress announcement unrelated to ticketing or live events.

### Key Points
- FTC is distributing 9,419 checks totaling over $672,000 to consumers harmed by Trend Deploy's deceptive marketing.
- Consumers should cash checks within 90 days; questions go to redress administrator JND Legal Administration.
- Case follows a 2025 court order requiring Frank Romero to pay the FTC for Mail Order Rule violations.
- No ticketing, resale, or live-events connection — included only because it arrived via the FTC press-release feed.
