# UTA Promotes James Wright To Co-Head Of UK Music

## Summary

Talent agency UTA has promoted James Wright to Co-Head of its UK Music group, where he will lead the London-based team alongside longtime co-head Neil Warnock. Wright joined UTA in 2015 via its acquisition of The Agency Group and represents artists including Lizzo, Take That and John Legend. The move follows his recent elevation to UTA Partner.

### Key Points
- James Wright promoted to Co-Head of UTA UK Music, alongside Neil Warnock.
- Wright joined UTA in 2015 through its acquisition of The Agency Group.
- His roster includes Lizzo, Take That, John Legend, Jacob Collier and Rita Ora.
- Follows his recent naming as a UTA Partner.

UTA has announced the promotion of James Wright to Co-Head of its UK Music group.

Wright, [a 2025 Pollstar Impact International Honoree](https://news.pollstar.com/2025/11/07/2025-impact-intl-uk-euro-honoree-james-wright/?_gl=1%2A1c720zc%2A_ga%2AMTM4MzMyNDM3Ny4xNzgyOTc2ODgw%2A_ga_5B6XRECYRM%2AczE3ODQxMDE4MzQkbzM3JGcxJHQxNzg0MTA2MTEyJGo2MCRsMCRoMA..), will lead the London-based music team alongside long-standing UK Co-Head, Neil Warnock, steering the department’s short and long-term strategies and overall industry positioning.

Wright joined UTA in 2015 following the company’s acquisition of The Agency Group. Prior to that, he was an agent at Elastic Artists. Before moving into the live music sector in 2009, Wright was A&R manager for Candid Records, where he signed acts, released records, and launched an in-house live booking agency offering 360-degree contracts to the label’s artists.

His roster spans pop, adult contemporary, jazz, electronic, and hip-hop, and includes artists such as Lizzo, Take That, John Legend, David Byrne, Jacob Collier, Underworld, The Marías, Rita Ora and Jon Batiste.

Wright’s promotion follows the [recent announcement that both he and fellow London Music Agent, Jules de Lattre have been made Partners at UTA](https://news.pollstar.com/2026/03/26/uta-names-35-partners-including-9-in-music-and-comedy/).

Last summer, UTA appointed Darnell Strom to lead its London office and the next phase of international growth and continued expansion into key markets across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

Over the past decade UTA’s built out teams in London across Music, Creators, Comedy, Brand & Advisory, Production Arts. Strategic investments including the acquisition of literary and talent powerhouse Curtis Brown and ROOF Football Agency.
