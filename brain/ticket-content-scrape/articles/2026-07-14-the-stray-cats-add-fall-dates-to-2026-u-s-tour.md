# The Stray Cats Add Fall Dates to 2026 U.S. Tour

## Summary

The Stray Cats have extended their 2026 U.S. tour with a newly announced fall leg running November 9 through December 6, bringing the total run to 37 shows between late July and early December. The new dates go on artist presale July 14, with general onsale via Ticketmaster beginning July 17.

### Key Points
- New fall leg runs Nov. 9 (Grand Prairie, TX) through Dec. 6 (Foxwoods, CT).
- Tour now totals 37 shows between late July and early December 2026.
- Artist presale begins July 14; general onsale July 17 via Ticketmaster.
- Summer leg continues through mid-August across western/midwestern U.S. venues.


## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/The-Stray-Cats.webp)

The Stray Cats have announced additional dates for their 2026 U.S. tour, extending the schedule through early December.

The newly announced leg begins November 9 in Grand Prairie, Texas, and includes stops in San Antonio, Nashville, Charlotte, Orlando, Atlanta and several other cities. The tour is set to wrap up on December 6 at Foxwoods Resort Casino in Mashantucket, Connecticut.

Before the fall run begins, the band will perform a previously announced series of summer concerts across the western, midwestern and northwestern United States. The itinerary includes stops in Las Vegas, Phoenix, Colorado Springs, Atlantic City and other cities. With the newly announced shows, the tour now includes 37 performances scheduled between late July and early December.

Tickets for the new dates will first be available through an artist presale beginning Tuesday, July 14, at 10 a.m. local time. General ticket sales will begin Friday, July 17, at 10 a.m. local time through Ticketmaster.

For more information and additional details, fans can visit [The Stray Cats’ official website](https://straycats.com/home/).
