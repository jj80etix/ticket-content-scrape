# Broadway’s ‘Six’ to Welcome Chloe Tucker Caine and Stephanie Jae Park

## Summary

Broadway's "Six" is adding Chloe Tucker Caine (Broadway debut, as Anne Boleyn) and Stephanie Jae Park (as Catherine Parr) to its cast starting August 17, replacing departing cast members Kirstin Maldonado and Anna Uzele.

### Key Points
- Chloe Tucker Caine joins as Anne Boleyn, replacing Kirstin Maldonado (final performance Aug. 9); Broadway debut.
- Stephanie Jae Park joins as Catherine Parr, replacing Anna Uzele (departed July 5).
- Both begin performances August 17, running through February 14, 2027.
- At the Lena Horne Theatre; production has run since 2020.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Six-The-Musical.webp)

The Broadway production of “Six” is welcoming two new performers to its cast this summer, with Chloe Tucker Caine and Stephanie Jae Park set to join the musical at the Lena Horne Theatre.

Caine and Park will begin performances on August 17 and continue through February 14, 2027. Caine will make her Broadway debut as Anne Boleyn, taking over the role from Pentatonix member Kirstin Maldonado, who is scheduled to give her final performance on August 9.

Park will step into the role of Catherine Parr following Anna Uzele’s departure from the production after her final performance on July 5.

The Broadway production of “Six” opened in 2020 and follows the six wives of Henry VIII as they reclaim their stories through a pop-inspired musical competition. Created by Toby Marlow and Lucy Moss, the production blends historical events with contemporary music influences.
