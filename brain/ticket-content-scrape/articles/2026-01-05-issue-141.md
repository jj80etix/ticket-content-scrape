# Issue 141

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's Issue 141 landing page — a table of contents for the January 2026 issue. Featured content includes the Oasis Live '25 reunion tour as IQ's Tour of the Year, agent Kirk Sommer's 25 years at WME, production veteran Dale "Opie" Skjerseth's Gaffer Award, the music/sport crossover trend, and a France market report.

### Key Points
- "Brotherly Live" retrospective on the Oasis reunion tour, named IQ's Tour of the Year, reuniting Noel and Liam Gallagher after 16 years.
- Profile of WME agent Kirk Sommer marking his 25-year career.
- Dale "Opie" Skjerseth profiled as 2025's recipient of The Gaffer Award.
- "Pitch Perfect" examines the growing trend of music-and-sport crossover events.
- Feature on Australia's Untitled Group (10 years) and a France market-focus report.
- Extracts include UTA's Darnell Strom on the firm's UK expansion and Superstruct CEO Alex Mahon on live experiences.
