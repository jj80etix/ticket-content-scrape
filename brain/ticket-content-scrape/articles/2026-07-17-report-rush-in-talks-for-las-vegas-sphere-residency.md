# Report: Rush in Talks for Las Vegas Sphere Residency

## Summary

Multiple reports suggest Rush is negotiating a Las Vegas Sphere residency for 2027, with one outlet describing a possible 16-to-18-show run and contracts potentially finalized within weeks. Neither Rush nor Sphere has confirmed the residency, and no dates or ticketing details exist yet.

### Key Points
- Reports cite unnamed sources describing active negotiations for a 2027 Sphere residency.
- Rock radio host Eddie Trunk separately claimed the deal was "done," citing 16-18 shows.
- Would extend Rush's ongoing "Fifty Something" reunion tour, its first major run since 2015.
- No official confirmation, dates, or ticketing information yet.

## Rumored Sphere Shows Would Extend Massive Rush Tour

A Las Vegas engagement would add another significant chapter to Rush’s already extensive “Fifty Something” reunion tour.

Geddy Lee and Alex Lifeson [announced the tour in October 2025](https://www.ticketnews.com/2025/10/rush-announce-fifty-something-2026-headline-tour/), marking their first major run together since Rush completed its R40 Tour in 2015. German drummer Anika Nilles joined Lee and Lifeson for the new iteration of the band following the 2020 death of longtime drummer and lyricist Neil Peart.
