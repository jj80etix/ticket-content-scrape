# Issue 143

## Summary

This is a table-of-contents preview for IQ Magazine Issue 143 (March 2026), previewing the global rise of country-music touring, a profile of veteran agent Nigel Hassler's 40-year career, a Biffy Clyro tour report, a market report on Spain's live-music industry, and a feature on private aviation logistics for global touring. The piece is a promotional issue summary linking to the magazine's full articles rather than standalone reporting with extractable facts.

### Key Points
- Feature story: country music's global export growth and international market expansion.
- Profile: agent Nigel Hassler marking 40 years in the touring business.
- Tour report: Biffy Clyro's "Futique" tour and fanbase growth across generations.
- Market report: Spain's live-music industry as a touring/festival hub and gateway for Latin American artists into Europe.
- Feature: private aviation logistics supporting global touring ("High Fidelity").
- Additional linked pieces cover Milan's live-music scene, African touring/venues, and an AI commentary Q&A.
