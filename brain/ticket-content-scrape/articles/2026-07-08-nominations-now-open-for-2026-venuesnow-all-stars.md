# Nominations Now Open For 2026 VenuesNow All-Stars

## Summary

Pollstar has opened nominations for the 2026 VenuesNow All-Star Awards, which recognize top executives, companies, and events shaping the venue business. Nominations are open through July 10, 2026, with honorees selected by Pollstar's editorial team using industry input and Pollstar's box office data, and will be celebrated at the VenuesNow Conference (Sept. 29-30, Sheraton Grand Seattle).

### Key Points
- Nominations for the 2026 VenuesNow All-Stars are open now through July 10, 2026 via a SurveyMonkey link.
- Award criteria include best-in-class venue operations, programming, development, hospitality, and design.
- Honorees are chosen by Pollstar's editorial team with industry input, informed by Pollstar's box office data.
- Winners will be celebrated at the VenuesNow Conference, Sept. 29-30 at the Sheraton Grand Seattle.
- Last year's cover honoree was Las Vegas Raiders President Sandra Douglass Morgan for her role with Allegiant Stadium.
