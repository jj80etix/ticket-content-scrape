# ‘Love Actually Live in Concert’ Announces 2026 U.S. Tour

## Summary

"Love Actually Live in Concert" will return for a 2026 U.S. holiday tour spanning 30 cities, pairing screenings of the 2003 film with a live orchestra performing Craig Armstrong's score. The tour runs from November 15 in Williamsport, PA through December 27 in Sugar Land, TX. General public tickets go on sale July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.

### Key Points
- Tour begins November 15, 2026 at Journey Bank Community Arts Center in Williamsport, PA.
- Tour concludes December 27, 2026 at Smart Financial Centre in Sugar Land, TX.
- Stops include Washington D.C., Atlantic City, Atlanta, Brooklyn, Boston, Chicago, Denver, Los Angeles, New Orleans, and Dallas, among others.
- Some venues (Atlantic City, Boston, Sugar Land) host multiple shows or performances on the same date.
- General on-sale begins Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.
- More information is available at loveactuallyinconcert.com.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Love-Actually-Live-in-Concert.webp)

The “Love Actually Live in Concert Tour” will return in 2026 with performances scheduled across 30 cities in the United States during the holiday season.

The 2026 tour is set to begin on November 15 in Williamsport, Pennsylvania at Journey Bank Community Arts Center. From there, the run is scheduled to make stops in cities such as Washington D.C., Atlantic City, Atlanta, Brooklyn, Boston, Chicago, Denver, Los Angeles, New Orleans and Dallas before wrapping up on December 27 in Sugar Land, Texas at Smart Financial Centre.

The tour will feature screenings of Richard Curtis’ 2003 romantic comedy *Love Actually* accompanied by a live orchestra performing Craig Armstrong’s score. The film will be projected on a large screen while the orchestra plays the music in sync with the movie.

Tickets for the 2026 “Love Actually Live in Concert Tour” will go on sale to the general public on Friday, July 24, at 10 a.m. local time.
