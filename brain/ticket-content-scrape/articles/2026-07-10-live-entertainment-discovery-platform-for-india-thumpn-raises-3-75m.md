# Live entertainment discovery platform for India ThumpN raises $3.75m

## Summary

Mumbai-based ThumpN, an AI-native live-entertainment discovery and ticketing platform for India, raised over $3.75m in a pre-seed round from investors including Paytm founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma's family office, Paytm's CFO, and singer Arijit Singh. Founded by ex-Paytm Insider COO Varun Khare, the platform's AI assistant "Shadow" recommends events via a conversational interface, aiming to solve fragmented event discovery across India's estimated 40,000 live events.

### Key Points
- $3.75m+ pre-seed round; investors include Paytm-linked backers and singer Arijit Singh.
- Founded by Varun Khare, former COO of Paytm Insider.
- AI assistant "Shadow" gives conversational, curator-network-assisted event recommendations.
- Positions purely as a discovery layer — explicitly not competing with promoters/organizers on content ("we don't want to produce or create our own IPs").
- Targets India's ~40,000 annual live events, where discovery today relies on social media, messaging apps and word-of-mouth.

### Highlights
- $3.75m pre-seed with notable strategic angel backing (Paytm ecosystem, a major recording artist) signals investor confidence in AI-driven discovery as a distinct layer from ticketing/production.
- Deliberately scoped as discovery-only (not producing content or competing with organizers) — a "picks-and-shovels" positioning.

### Industry Problem
Event discovery in large, fragmented live markets (India's ~40,000 events/year) is unsolved — patrons rely on informal channels, and organizers/venues lack an aggregation layer connecting demand to their events.

### Proposed Solution
ThumpN's conversational AI recommendation layer is a direct build/benchmark reference for Etix Assistant: an AI-native discovery/recommendation surface (event matching via conversational interface) is emerging as a differentiator distinct from core ticketing, and could be evaluated as an Etix Assistant capability for patron-facing discovery.
