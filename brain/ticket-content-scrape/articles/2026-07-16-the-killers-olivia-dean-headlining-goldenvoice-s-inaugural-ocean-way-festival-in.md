# The Killers, Olivia Dean Headlining Goldenvoice’s Inaugural Ocean Way Festival In Santa Monica

## Summary

Goldenvoice announced the inaugural Ocean Way Festival, a two-day event on Santa Monica Beach headlined by The Killers and Olivia Dean, marking the promoter's return to Santa Monica and the first major music festival staged on the city's beach. The festival runs September 26-27, with a Santa Monica-resident presale beginning July 22 and general on-sale July 23.

### Key Points
- Dates: Sept. 26-27, on Santa Monica Beach next to the pier.
- Headliners: The Killers and Olivia Dean; lineup also includes Jack White, Khruangbin, Sublime, and Hiatus Kaiyote.
- Santa Monica-resident presale begins July 22 at 11 a.m. PT; general public on-sale is July 23 at 11 a.m. PT.
- Produced by Goldenvoice; VP of regional festivals Nic Adler cites the venue area's Civic Auditorium and Ocean Way Recording legacy.
- Festival includes a paid hospitality add-on, the Proper Beach Club, via partnership with Proper Hospitality (CEO Brad Korzen).
- Passes and hospitality tickets are sold via oceanwayfestival.com and hospitality.oceanwayfestival.com.

Goldenvoice is officially back in Santa Monica. The promoter, whose roots are on the West Coast with legendary rock shows at the Civic Auditorium, announced the inaugural Ocean Way Festival, and it will be headlined by The Killers and Olivia Dean.

The two-day event takes place Sept. 26-27 and is the first major music festival on Santa Monica Beach, with the iconic pier framing the main stage. Ocean Way celebrates music, food, wellness, design, sustainability and beach culture on the California coast with a stacked lineup that features Jack White, Khruangbin, Sublime, Durand Jones and the Indications, Alvvays, SG Lewis, 54 Ultra, Hiatus Kaiyote, BLOND:ISH, Poolside, Hot Chip DJs and more. See full lineup below.

**[Why Santa Monica Is Upping Its Live Music Game](https://news.pollstar.com/2026/04/24/fest-411-live-music-could-boost-santa-monicas-realignment-plan/)**

“Santa Monica has an incredible music history, and Goldenvoice has been fortunate to be part of that story,” said Nic Adler, Goldenvoice vice president of regional festivals. “From legendary shows at the Civic Auditorium to the origins of Ocean Way Recording just steps from the beach, there’s a creative legacy here that’s unlike anywhere else. Ocean Way Festival is our opportunity to celebrate that history while building something that feels authentically Santa Monica for years to come.”

Fans can register for passes now at [oceanwayfestival.com](https://oceanwayfestival.com/). Santa Monica residents have access to a presale beginning July 22 at 11 a.m. PT. Tickets go on sale to the general public July 23 at 11 a.m. PT.

Hotel packages are available, and the festival also introduces the Proper Beach Club, a beachfront hospitality experience designed in partnership with Proper Beach Club. Those tickets are now available at [hospitality.oceanwayfestival.com](https://hospitality.oceanwayfestival.com/).

“Music and design have always been at the core of what we do at Proper. Proper Presents has been a big part of that journey, and the Proper Beach Club at Ocean Way Festival feels like a natural next step,” said Brad Korzen, CEO of Proper Hospitality. “To bring our approach to hospitality to an event of this scale, right here in Santa Monica, takes everything we’ve built to a whole new level.”
