# Goose Announces 13th Annual Goosemas at Jim Whelan Boardwalk Hall

## Summary

Goose announced its 13th annual Goosemas holiday celebration for Dec. 11-12 at Jim Whelan Boardwalk Hall in Atlantic City, marking the band's final shows of 2026. Presales begin July 22 with public on-sale July 24, and the band continues its partnership with the Western Sun Foundation, donating $1 from every Goosemas ticket sold.

### Key Points
- Goosemas 2026 set for Dec. 11-12 at Jim Whelan Boardwalk Hall, Atlantic City.
- Caps a fall tour running August through November across US venues including Red Rocks and The Greek Theatre.
- Artist presale begins July 22; public on-sale July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.
- $1 from every Goosemas ticket benefits the Western Sun Foundation.
- Band also has a September Bourbon & Beyond date and a January 2027 Riviera Maya festival appearance with Dave Matthews & Tim Reynolds.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Goosmas.webp)

Goose has announced the return of Goosemas, with the band’s 13th annual holiday celebration scheduled for December 11 and 12 at Jim Whelan Boardwalk Hall in Atlantic City, New Jersey.

The two-night event will mark Goose’s final performances of 2026. The concerts follow the band’s tour, which runs from August through November and includes stops at venues across the United States.

The upcoming tour features performances at The Greek Theatre in Los Angeles, WAMU Theater in Seattle, Red Rocks Amphitheatre near Denver, The Midland Theatre in Kansas City, The Truth in Nashville, and Citizens Live at the Wylie in Pittsburgh.

Tickets for Goosemas will first be available through the band’s official presale. Fans can register for presale access at goosetheband.com/tour and will receive a unique code to participate. The official artist presale begins on July 22 at 10 a.m. local time.

Local presales will begin later that day at 12 p.m. local time. All presales will end on July 23 at 10 p.m. local time. Tickets will go on sale to the general public on July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.

Goose will also continue its partnership with the Western Sun Foundation through this year’s Goosemas event. One dollar from every ticket sold for the two performances will be donated to the nonprofit organization.

Additionally, Goose is also slated to perform at [Bourbon & Beyond](https://www.ticketnews.com/2026/02/bourbon-beyond-reveals-2026-lineup-led-by-foo-fighters-chris-stapleton-and-mumford-sons/) on September 24 in Louisville. Looking ahead to 2027, the band is scheduled to perform two sets at [“Dave Matthews & Tim Reynolds: 10 Years on the Beach,”](https://www.ticketnews.com/2026/04/dave-matthews-and-tim-reynolds-announce-2027-riviera-maya-10th-anniversary-lineup/) taking place January 20 to 23 at Moon Palace in Riviera Maya, Mexico.
