# LA28 releases ‘once in a lifetime’ ticket packages

## Summary

On Location, the official hospitality provider for the LA28 Olympic Games, has released a new wave of premium hospitality ticket packages covering equestrian, diving, athletics, beach volleyball, and golf, with additional equestrian, football, and basketball packages going on sale July 28. Packages range from view-focused lounges to private suites, including a new "Follow My Team" concept guaranteeing seats across multiple rounds for basketball fans.

### Key Points
- New On Location hospitality packages cover equestrian (Santa Anita Park), diving (Rose Bowl Aquatics Center), athletics (LA Memorial Coliseum), beach volleyball (Long Beach), and golf (Riviera Country Club).
- Additional equestrian, football, and basketball packages go on sale July 28.
- "Follow My Team" packages for men's and women's basketball guarantee seats at three preliminary rounds plus one quarterfinal match.
- On Location president Paul Caine describes the packages as "immersive journeys" designed to go beyond a standard ticket.
- The LA28 Games run July 14-30.

## Transcript

![TheTicketingBusiness News](https://www.theticketingbusiness.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/TBN-LOGO-reversed-white.png)

TheTicketingBusiness News

**Official hospitality provider for the Los Angeles 2028 Olympic Games, On Location, has launched a new wave of “once in a lifetime” ticket packages for the event.**

Hospitality packages for equestrian and diving are now available, alongside newly released package types for athletics, beach volleyball, and golf.

The beach volleyball premium lounge will offer 360-degree views of the court on Long Beach and guests will receive continuous food and beverage service.

The diving leisure lounge at the Rose Bowl Aquatics Center allows guests to enjoy mountain views in a semi-covered lounge with light bites and a welcome drink, but does not include direct views of the field of play.

The equestrian signature lounge at Santa Anita Park includes a private, reserved table along with beverage service, cuisine, and field-of-play views.

Private suites at Riviera Country Club, located on the upper level of the 18th hole structure, are said to be the best of the golf offerings at the Games.

At LA Memorial Coliseum, the athletics loge boxes offer open-air views of the competition area as well as access to the leisure lounge with premium food and drink options.

Additional packages for equestrian, football and basketball are also set to go on sale on July 28.

For example, Follow My Team packages for men’s and women’s basketball will guarantee a spot at three preliminary rounds and one quarter-final round match.

“The LA28 Olympic Games is the perfect opportunity to create once-in-a-lifetime hospitality experiences for fans,” said Paul Caine, president of On Location.

“These newly released packages span some of the most exciting sports at the Olympic Games and are crafted to give fans unprecedented proximity to the competition.

“From curated lounges and private suites to the new ‘Follow My Team’ concept for men’s and women’s basketball, we’re designing hospitality experiences that go far beyond a ticket; they’re immersive journeys that make every moment of the Olympic Games feel personal, effortless and extraordinary.”

The Los Angeles 2028 Olympics will take place from July 14-30.
