# Amazon Music To Exclusively Livestream Vans Warped Tour In Long Beach

## Summary

Amazon Music will exclusively livestream the Vans Warped Tour's Long Beach return (July 25-26) across its app, Twitch, Prime Video and connected-TV channels, in partnership with Insomniac.

### Key Points
- Exclusive livestream of Vans Warped Tour, July 25-26, Long Beach.
- Available on Amazon Music app, Twitch, Prime Video, Fire TV and Samsung TV Plus.
- 100+ acts including Jimmy Eat World, Taking Back Sunday, Simple Plan, Third Eye Blind, Bowling for Soup.
- Produced in partnership with Insomniac.

The return of the Vans Warped Tour to Long Beach will be exclusively livestreamed on Amazon Music. The beloved rock festival takes place July 25-26 at the Downtown Long Beach Shoreline Waterfront with more than 100 acts performing, including Jimmy Eat World, Taking Back Sunday, Simple Plan, Third Eye Blind, Bowling for Soup and more.

Fans can watch the festival celebrating punk, alternative and rock on the Amazon Music app as well as on the Amazon Music channels on Twitch and Prime Video. Those with Fire TV and Samsung TV Plus can tune in on the Amazon Live Channel.

The two-day event is produced in partnership with Insomniac and builds on the legacy of the festival that gave a platform to today’s biggest punk, emo and rock acts. In addition to the bands performing, there will be appearances from world-class skateboarders and action sports athletes.

Amazon Music’s livestream will be hosted by rock musician Lilith Czar (aka Juliet Simms), Caity Babs of SiriusXM and Andy Biersack of Black Veil Brides. They will interview festival artists and preview some of the notable performances throughout the weekend. Fans can prepare for the event by listening to the [official 2026 Vas Warped Tour playlist on Amazon Music](https://music.amazon.com/playlists/B0H7YH4GGD?tag=linkfiregen&ie=UTF8&linkCode=as2&ascsubtag=b5c8fbb353276c4892dfa75e5df1ccad&ref=dmm_acq_soc_us_u_lfire_lp_x_b5c8fbb353276c4892dfa75e5df1ccad).

For more information about the Vans Warped Tour in Long Beach, visit [www.vanswarpedtourlongbeach.com](https://www.vanswarpedtourlongbeach.com/).
