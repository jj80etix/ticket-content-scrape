# People on the move: Minnesota Wild and more…

## Summary

TheTicketingBusiness's regular "People on the move" column covers hiring and promotion news across the ticketing and live-events sector, led by Minnesota Wild/MSE promoting Erica McKenzie to VP of ticket sales and service. Additional moves cover AEG Presents' venue-development team, a new Night Time Industries Association (Australia) chief executive, and hires at TicketManager and Brentford FC.

### Key Points
- Erica McKenzie promoted to VP of ticket sales and service at Minnesota Wild/Minnesota Sports & Entertainment (MSE), having served as senior director since 2022.
- AEG Presents expands its North America venue-development team: Cameron Marcotte to VP of venue development; Jake Hiersteiner and Mike Ryan promoted to senior director and project director respectively; Alyssa Sarilarp, Will Evans and Marysol Flores join in design/construction and project-management roles.
- Paul McLeay named CEO of Australia's Night Time Industries Association, moving over from the Australian Distillers Association.
- Casey Colby joins TicketManager as strategic account director; Oliver Lawrenson becomes ticketing operations lead at Premier League club Brentford.

TheTicketingBusiness News

**We round up the latest movements from the ticketing sector and associated industries…**

Minnesota Sports & Entertainment (MSE) and the NHL’s Minnesota Wild have promoted **Erica McKenzie** to vice president of ticket sales and service.

McKenzie has served as a senior director of ticket sales and service since 2022, and under her leadership, the organisation has driven growth in season ticket memberships while elevating the overall customer experience.

“Throughout her career, Erica has become a respected leader known for building and leading high-performing teams that consistently exceed sales and retention revenue goals while elevating the fan experience,” said senior vice president of ticket sales and service Maria Troje.

“A passionate mentor and champion of professional development, Erica is committed to cultivating talent and driving the future of sports ticketing through continuous innovation, service excellence and a people-first leadership approach.”

AEG Presents has made a number of changes and additions to its venue development team in North America.

Internal promotions include **Cameron Marcotte**, who has been elevated to vice president of venue development, while  **Jake Hiersteiner**  and  **Mike Ryan** have been promoted to the roles of senior director of venue development, and project director of venue development, respectively.

Meanwhile, **Alyssa Sarilarp**  has joined the company as senior design and construction specialist, and  **Will Evans**  has taken on the role of senior project manager. **Marysol Flores** has joined as administrative assistant.

**Paul McLeay** has been appointed as the chief executive of Australia’s Night Time Industries Association.

He joins the industry body after serving as chief executive of the Australian Distillers Association.

Elsewhere, **Casey Colby**  has joined TicketManager as strategic account director, and  **Oliver Lawrenson** has taken on the new role of ticketing operations lead at Premier League club Brentford.
