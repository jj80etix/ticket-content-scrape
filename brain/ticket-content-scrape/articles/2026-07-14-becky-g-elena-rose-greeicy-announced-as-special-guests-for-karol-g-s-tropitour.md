# Becky G, Elena Rose, Greeicy Announced As Special Guests For Karol G’s ‘Tropitour’

## Summary

Karol G's upcoming North American stadium tour, kicking off July 24 in Chicago, will feature Becky G, Elena Rose, and Greeicy as supporting guests on select dates. The piece details which cities each artist will appear in alongside their own separate touring and release plans.

### Key Points
- Becky G joins 10 dates including a hometown SoFi Stadium show on Aug. 15; new album BARAJA BENDITA drops Aug. 14.
- Elena Rose joins 10 U.S. stadium dates including the Chicago kickoff shows July 24-25.
- Greeicy supports three dates (Inglewood, Houston, Miami) ahead of her own "Candela World Tour."
- Tour opens at Chicago's Soldier Field July 24.

Karol G has one of the most anticipated tours of the year kicking off in less than two weeks, and she’s bringing a few special guests with her. Becky G, Elena Rose and Greeicy will support select North American dates of the Colombian superstar’s ambitious stadium trek, which kicks off in Chicago on July 24.

Becky G will perform at 10 of Karol G’s concerts, including a hometown show at SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, California, on Aug. 15. She will also make appearances for shows in Toronto, Washington, D.C., San Francisco, Seattle, San Antonio, El Paso and New Jersey.

The Mexican American star joined Karol G for her memorable Coachella set earlier this year. Becky G recently released new music, “MARATHON”, “EPA” AND “PATRONA,” and announced her new album *BARAJA BENDITA* drops Aug. 14.

Venezuelan American singer Elena Rose, who is currently on the European leg of her own tour, will also join Karol G for 10 U.S. stadium dates, including the tour kickoff shows at Chicago’s Soldier Field July 24 and 25. Rose will also perform in Las Vegas, Inglewood, Boston, New Jersey, Atlanta, Miami, Tampa and Dallas.

Rose recently participated in the FIFA World Cup 2026 celebrations, performing a countdown concert in Mexico City.

Colombian pop star Greeicy will support three dates of the “Tropitour,” performing in Inglewood on Aug. 16, Houston on Sept. 27 and Miami on Oct. 3. The announcement comes days before Greeicy launches her “Candela World Tour,” which kicks off at Cook Music Fest in Tenerife and has the artist visiting the U.K., Canada, Mexico, Peru, Argentina, Costa Rica and more.

For more information about Karol G’s tour, visit [karolgmusic.com](https://shopkarolg.com/pages/tour).
