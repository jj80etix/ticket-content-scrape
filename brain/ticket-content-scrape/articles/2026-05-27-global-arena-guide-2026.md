# Global Arena Guide 2026

## Summary

This is a promotional table-of-contents for IQ Magazine's "Global Arena Guide 2026" report, covering 70 country market profiles and a directory of 630+ arenas worldwide for tour-planning purposes. It also highlights the new reader-nominated "IQ Arena Stars 2026" list of 20 best-in-class venues, an "Arena Fever" feature on upcoming venue openings, and linked executive Q&As from venue operators.

### Key Points
- Report profiles arena markets across 70 countries, covering every continent except Antarctica.
- Includes a directory of 630+ (635) arenas with contact information for tour planning.
- Introduces "IQ Arena Stars 2026," a reader-nominated list of 20 best-in-class venues.
- "Arena Fever" section covers upcoming arena openings around the world.
- Includes executive Q&As from AEG International, Unipol Dome (Italy), the European Arenas Association/UK National Arenas Association, and ASM Global.
