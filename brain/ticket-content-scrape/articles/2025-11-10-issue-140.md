# Issue 140

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's landing/preview page for print Issue 140 (November 2025), listing its cover stories rather than presenting their content. Featured pieces include the "Indie Champions 2025" list, a "State of Independents" piece on consolidation pressures, a profile of manager Natasha Gregory, a tour report on Architects' European arena run, and a 20th-anniversary feature on Luxembourg venue Rockhal.

### Key Points
- Page promotes IQ Magazine print Issue 140 (November 2025), with links to subscribe or buy the print edition.
- Cover feature: "Indie Champions 2025," honoring independent companies in the live sector.
- "State of Independents" examines why professionals choose to stay independent amid industry consolidation, noting agency merger ROAM (Arrival Artists + ATC Live) as a case study.
- Profile: Natasha Gregory's 20 years in music and the fifth anniversary of Mother Artists.
- Tour report on Architects' European arena tour, and a feature marking Rockhal venue's 20th anniversary.
- No substantive article body text was captured — only headlines, teaser copy, and navigation links to other issues/reports.
