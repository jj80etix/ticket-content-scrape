# Issue 144

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's Issue 144 landing page — a table of contents for the May 2026 issue. Featured content includes Olivia Dean's first arena tour, promoter John Giddings' 50-year career, Yungblud's "B.R.A.T." artist-development hub in London, a Switzerland market report, and a Manchester city focus.

### Key Points
- "The Art of Loving Live" goes behind the scenes of Olivia Dean's first arena tour.
- "Flying Solo" profiles John Giddings marking 50 years in the live-music business.
- "The B.R.A.T. Pack" explores Yungblud's London artist-development base and whether the model can scale.
- Market Report: Switzerland's affluent, sophisticated live-entertainment audience.
- City Focus: Manchester's cultural and economic resurgence.
- Additional pieces cover extended trading hours for live-music venues and country music's stadium-touring growth.
