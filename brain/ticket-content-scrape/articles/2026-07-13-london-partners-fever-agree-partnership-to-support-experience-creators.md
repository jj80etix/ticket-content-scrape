# London & Partners, Fever agree partnership to support experience creators

## Summary

London & Partners, the city's growth agency, partnered with global experiences platform Fever to help event/experience creators launch and scale in London, connecting them with venues, audiences, and ticketing expertise. The deal supports London's "experience economy" growth plan, an industry Fever pegs at $5.2tn globally.

### Key Points
- London & Partners and Fever will share market insights and support international operators entering London.
- Fever is known for Candlelight Concerts, The Friends Experience, and other ticketed pop-up experiences.
- Part of London's official "Growth Plan" strategy targeting the experience economy.
- Fever cites the global experience economy as worth $5.2tn, contributing over £130bn to the UK economy.

**London & Partners, the UK capital’s growth agency, has teamed up with global discovery platform Fever to aid experience creators in launching, growing and reaching new audiences in the city.**

The announcement forms part of London & Partners’ commitment to growing the capital’s experience economy, a key growth sector in the London Growth Plan, by supporting experience companies.

Fever is behind popular experiences such as Candlelight Concerts, The Friends Experience, Jurassic World: The Exhibition – and opening later this month, TimeWalk The Exhibition at Immerse LDN.

Through the partnership, London & Partners and Fever will bring together industry leaders, share market insights, support international operators considering entering London, and connect creators with the venues, audiences, and ticketing expertise they need to launch and grow.

**Land, grow and succeed**

“If you are a creator looking to bring an experience to London, partnerships like this make it easier to land, grow, and succeed,” said Simon McCaugherty, director of experience economy at London & Partners.

“From finding the right venue and reaching the right audiences, to getting marketing and ticketing right, this work is often behind the scenes, but crucial to making London the top choice for the next generation of experience creators.”

According to Fever, the global experience economy – which spans hospitality, culture, events and tourism – is worth $5.2tn (£3.9tn/€4.5tn), contributing more than £130bn to the UK economy alone.

“Announcing our partnership with London & Partners is a great moment for Fever in the UK,” added Sana Ali Aarmir, general manager for Fever and Secret Media Network in Europe.

“London is a cultural melting pot which competes on a global scale for the best experiences, making it a key location for us.

“By combining Fever’s understanding of what audiences want and where the industry is heading with London & Partners’ comprehensive knowledge of the London ecosystem, we’re looking forward to helping more creators choose London as the home for their next world-class experience.”
