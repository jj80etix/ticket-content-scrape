# Morris Higham Management Appoints Clark Mims Tedesco As Vice President, Marketing

## Summary

Artist development company Morris Higham Management (MHM) has appointed Clark Mims Tedesco as vice president of marketing, expanding its executive leadership team. Tedesco joins after a 13-year run at Warner Records Nashville, where she led marketing and strategy for MHM flagship client Kenny Chesney, including the campaigns for his *Here And Now* (2020) and *BORN* (2024) albums. She also has a decade of experience in New York's Broadway community.

### Key Points
- MHM names Clark Mims Tedesco VP of Marketing.
- She previously spent 13 years at Warner Records Nashville leading Kenny Chesney's marketing and strategy.
- Also led campaigns for Blake Shelton, Cody Johnson, Dan + Shay, Hudson Westbrook and others at Warner.
- Nearly a decade of prior experience in NYC's Broadway community.
- At MHM she will lead marketing ecosystem development for the roster, including Chesney, Old Dominion, and developing artists.

Artist development company Morris Higham Management (MHM) expanded its executive leadership team by appointing Clark Mims Tedesco as vice president, marketing.

Tedesco is a seasoned professional who previously served a 13-year tenure at Warner Records Nashville, where she was instrumental in leading marketing and strategy for Kenny Chesney, HGM’s flagship client. Tedesco was pivotal to the success of the artist’s 2020 album *Here And Now* and 2024’s *BORN*. She has also led campaigns for Blake Shelton, Cody Johnson, Dan + Shay, Hudson Westbrook, Ingrid Andress and many more at Warner Records Nashville.

“Clark is a strategist who understands the nuance of building authentic, lasting connections between artists and their global audiences,” said Kyle Quigley, general manager of MHM. “Her leadership and relentless drive for innovation make her a vital addition as we continue to evolve at MHM.”

Tedesco also worked for nearly a decade in New York City’s Broadway community, and that experience, along with her work at Warner, has helped her develop a collaborative spirit and fierce competitiveness aimed at upholding traditions while pushing creative and commercial boundaries.

At MHM, Tedesco will lead the development of marketing ecosystems for the company’s roster, including Chesney, Old Dominion, Barbara Mandrell, Louise Mandrell, Daily & Vincent, Greylan James, Kinsey and Brad Tursi.
