# BTS, Bruno Mars, Zach Bryan Lead Gillette Stadium’s 2026 Concert Lineup

## Summary

Gillette Stadium's 2026 concert series adds BTS, Bruno Mars, Karol G, Ed Sheeran, Zach Bryan, and a Chris Brown/Usher co-headline show — six acts across 10 shows from August through October, with several stadium debuts.

### Key Points
- BTS opens the series Aug. 5–6 with an in-the-round stage design.
- Ed Sheeran's Sept. 25–26 shows mark his 8th and 9th stadium appearances.
- Series runs through Zach Bryan's Oct. 2–3 closing dates.

## Transcript

![Gillette Stadium | Photo courtesy David Silverman / Kraft Sports and Entertainment](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/Untitled-design-79-1.jpg)

Gillette Stadium | Photo courtesy David Silverman / Kraft Sports and Entertainment

Gillette Stadium is gearing up for a blockbuster stretch of concerts from big-name artists, including BTS, Bruno Mars, Karol G, Ed Sheeran, Zach Bryan and a co-headlining show from Chris Brown and USHER set to take over Foxborough this summer and fall.

The 2026 Gillette Stadium Concert Series presented by Dunkin’ will feature six musical acts performing 10 concerts from August through October, with several artists making their first appearances at the home of the New England Patriots and New England Revolution.

The run begins Aug. 5 and 6 with BTS, as the K-pop superstars launch the stadium’s concert season with two stops on the BTS World Tour ‘ARIRANG.’ The performances will mark the first and largest headlining K-pop shows in Gillette Stadium history and will feature a 360-degree, in-the-round stage designed to place fans at the center of the production.

Chris Brown and USHER will make their Gillette Stadium debuts Aug. 17 when their co-headlining R&B Tour arrives in Foxborough.

The stadium will temporarily swap concerts for Banana Ball on Aug. 28 and 29, when the Savannah Bananas face the Loco Beach Coconuts. The games will feature the league’s signature mix of baseball, choreographed dances, scoring celebrations, musical performances and other fan-focused entertainment.

Bruno Mars will kick off September with his first Gillette Stadium performances on Sept. 5 and 6. “The Romantic Tour” dates will also feature Anderson.Paak performing as DJ Pee.Wee, along with rising artist RAYE. The shows are part of Mars’ first full headlining tour in nearly a decade.

Karol G will return to Foxborough on Sept. 12 as part of her “Viajando Por El Mundo Tropitour.” The performance marks her first Gillette Stadium appearance since 2023.

Ed Sheeran follows with two “LOOP Tour” concerts on Sept. 25 and 26, joined by Macklemore, Lukas Graham and Aaron Rowe. The dates will mark Sheeran’s eighth and ninth performances at Gillette Stadium, which was the first NFL venue he headlined. His 2023 appearance set the stadium’s current single-show attendance record.
