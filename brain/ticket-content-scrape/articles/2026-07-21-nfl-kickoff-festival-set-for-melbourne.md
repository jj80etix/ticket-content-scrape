# NFL Kickoff Festival set for Melbourne

## Summary

Live Nation Australia and the NFL will run a three-day Kickoff Festival at Melbourne Park (Sept. 10–12) around the 49ers-Rams regular-season game at the MCG, Australia's first NFL regular-season hosting. General on-sale for festival tickets (A$49–A$59) begins July 29.

### Key Points
- Festival runs Sept. 10–12 at Melbourne Park; game is Sept. 11 at the MCG.
- General on-sale opens July 29; kids 13 and under enter free.
- Programming includes an NFL commissioner Q&A and league legends.

TheTicketingBusiness News

**The NFL Kickoff Festival is set to take place in Melbourne this September to celebrate Australia’s first hosting of a regular season game.**

It will begin on September 10 and run for three days at Melbourne Park, with activities such as flag football sessions, an American food village, music concerts, and official watch parties.

It will surround the Melbourne Cricket Ground’s hosting of the match between the San Francisco 49ers and the Los Angeles Rams on September 11.

NFL Kickoff Festival general on sale tickets will be available to purchase from July 29, with Thursday and Saturday sessions priced at A$49 (£25/$35/€30) while the Friday costs A$59.

Children aged 13 and under will be able to access the Kickoff Festival for free on all three days.

“The NFL’s first regular season game in Australia is a landmark moment, and Live Nation is proud to help build the excitement around it with a game-week experience that matches the scale of the occasion,” said Luke Hede, senior vice president of touring and commercial at Live Nation Australia, as reported by [Billboard](https://www.billboard.com/pro/live-nation-nfl-melbourne-kickoff-festival/).

“The Kickoff Festival will bring together live music, entertainment and the unmistakable energy of the NFL, giving fans a new way to be part of the action. From Melbourne Park to the MCG, this will be a truly memorable celebration for fans across the city.”

NFL legends, pundits and executives will join the festival schedule, including a live Q&A with NFL commissioner Roger Goodell.

“The Kickoff Festival promises to be an action-packed experience for sports fans of all ages,” said NFL general manager for Australia and New Zealand Charlotte Offord.

“Whether fans are attending the game or not, this event is the perfect way to get a taste of the NFL, be part of the celebrations and enjoy what we know is going to be an incredible few days in Melbourne.”
