# Lucinda Williams Announces ‘World’s Gone Wrong’ Fall Tour Of North America

## Summary

Grammy winner Lucinda Williams has announced a 31-date theater tour of the U.S. and Canada in support of her "World's Gone Wrong" album, running September 24 through November 17, 2026. The tour includes multi-night stands in Washington D.C., Chicago, and Minneapolis. Williams is represented by High Road Touring's Frank Riley.

### Key Points
- 31-date tour begins September 24-25 in Atlanta and closes November 16-17 in Minneapolis.
- Multi-night runs in Washington D.C. (9:30 Club, Lincoln Theatre), Chicago (Old Town School of Folk Music), and Minneapolis (Dakota).
- Most recent reported gross was $82,773 on 1,497 tickets at Seattle's 5th Avenue Theater (Oct. 2025).
- Represented by Frank Riley of High Road Touring.

Multiple-time Grammy winner Lucinda Williams will tour the U.S. and Canada on the “World’s Gone Wrong Tour,” in support of the album of the same name.

The 31-date theater tour begins with a double at Atlanta’s Variety Playhouse Sept. 24 and 25. The run includes two nights in Washington, D.C. (at the 9:30 Club and Lincoln Theater). Williams will also play doubles in Woodstock, New York; Dallas and Toronto. The tour wraps with two sets of four-night stands: at Chicago’s Old Town School of Folk Music (Nov. 7-8 and 10-11) and Minneapolis’ Dakota (Nov. 13-14 and 16-17).

The most recently reported U.S. headline date for Williams in the Pollstar Boxoffice is an Oct. 9, 2025, show at Seattle’s 5th Avenue Theater where she grossed $82,773 on 1,497 tickets,

Williams is repped by High Road Touring’s Frank Riley in North America.
