# Darius Rucker, Post Malone and Bob Dylan Top MEGASeats Hot Concerts This Week

## Summary

MEGASeats' weekly hot-concert rankings for July 22-29 are led by Darius Rucker at Red Rocks Amphitheatre, with Post Malone/Jelly Roll and Bob Dylan shows also placing near the top. Red Rocks and Pacific Amphitheatre together account for nine of the top 20 events, in a week shaped by country, classic rock, and nostalgia-heavy package shows.

### Key Points
- Darius Rucker's July 25 Red Rocks show leads the week's rankings.
- ABBA LA tribute pairing ranks second, with an accessible ~$58 average ticket price.
- Post Malone and Jelly Roll's July 28 Salt Lake City date ranks third.
- Cincinnati Music Festival bill (Mary J. Blige, Trey Songz, SWV, and others) is a top entry.
- Nostalgia package tours (Sugar Ray/Smash Mouth/Tonic, Lionel Richie/Earth Wind & Fire, Lil Wayne/2 Chainz) round out the week's themes.

Fresh and Slick Rick. Several artists place more than once. Dylan’s July 24 and July 25 performances at Wolf Trap rank fifth and seventh, respectively, while [AC/DC](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/acdc-13?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) lands two stadium dates in the top 15: July 28 in Denver and July 24 in San Antonio. Sarah McLachlan with Allison Russell, Lauren Daigle, Dave Matthews Band, Jack White, Cypress Hill, Sombr and Cross Canadian Ragweed round out a list that crosses generations and genres.
