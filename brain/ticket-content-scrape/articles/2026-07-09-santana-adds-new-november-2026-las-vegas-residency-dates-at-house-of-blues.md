# Santana Adds New November 2026 Las Vegas Residency Dates at House of Blues

## Summary

Santana added eight new November 2026 dates to his Las Vegas House of Blues residency ("An Intimate Evening with Santana: Greatest Hits Live"), with presales beginning July 8-10 ahead of general public onsale July 11 via Ticketmaster.

### Key Points
- Eight new residency dates added: Nov. 4, 5, 7, 8, 11, 12, 14, 15 at House of Blues, Mandalay Bay.
- Citi cardmember and artist presales run July 8-9; Live Nation All Access presale July 10; general onsale July 11 via Ticketmaster.
- A portion of proceeds benefits an unspecified cause per the announcement.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Santana.webp)

Santana is expanding his 2026 Las Vegas residency with a newly announced run of November performances at the House of Blues inside Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino.

The additional shows will continue the band’s “An Intimate Evening with Santana: Greatest Hits Live: Presented by SiriusXM” residency series. The newly added performances are scheduled for November 4, 5, 7, 8, 11, 12, 14, and 15.

Ticket sales for the newly announced November 2026 shows will begin with a Citi cardmember presale on Wednesday, July 8, at 10 a.m. local time. An artist presale will follow at the same time on Thursday, July 9.

A Live Nation presale is scheduled to begin Friday, July 10, at 10 a.m. local time. Fans can access the presale by signing up for Live Nation’s free All Access membership. Tickets remaining after the presales will become available to the general public on Saturday, July 11, at 10 a.m. local time through Ticketmaster.

A portion of proceeds from ticket sales will benefit Santana’s The Milagro Foundation, a nonprofit organization founded by Santana and his family that supports underserved children through programs focused on education, health, and the arts. Additional proceeds will support the House of Blues Music Forward Foundation, which works to use music as a tool to help young people build meaningful and successful futures.

The announcement comes as Santana continues a busy touring schedule. The musician is currently performing on a co-headlining tour with The Doobie Brothers, with dates scheduled through late August. Following the tour, Santana will return to Las Vegas for previously announced September residency performances before beginning the newly revealed November run.
