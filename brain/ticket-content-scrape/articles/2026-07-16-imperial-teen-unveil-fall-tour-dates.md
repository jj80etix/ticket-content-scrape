# Imperial Teen Unveil Fall Tour Dates

## Summary

Indie pop band Imperial Teen has announced a fall headline tour spanning eight US cities, following two already sold-out San Francisco shows at the Castro Theatre in August. The band — Roddy Bottum, Will Schwartz, Jone Stebbins, and Lynn Truell — plays intimate venues starting October 14 in Los Angeles and wrapping November 7 in Durham, NC, with support from Kids with a Crime Spree on select dates.

### Key Points
- Fall tour kicks off October 14 at Zebulon in Los Angeles and ends November 7 at The Pinhook in Durham, NC.
- Sold-out August 22–23 shows at San Francisco's Castro Theatre will feature The Breeders as guests.
- Other stops include Bottom of the Hill (San Francisco), Barboza (Seattle), Swan Dive (Portland), The Lilypad (Cambridge), and Baby's All Right (Brooklyn).
- Band lineup: Roddy Bottum, Will Schwartz, Jone Stebbins, and Lynn Truell.
- Kids with a Crime Spree supports on select dates.
- Tickets available via the band's Bandsintown page.

In addition to two sold-out San Francisco concerts in August, Imperial Teen will be headlining shows in eight cities this fall, bringing their razor-sharp hooks and intricate harmonies to intimate venues.

The quartet, comprising Roddy Bottum, Will Schwartz, Jone Stebbins, and Lynn Truell, will kick off their fall tour Oct. 14 at Zebulon in Los Angeles, followed by a hometown show in San Francisco at Bottom of the Hill. Imperial Teen will also visit Seattle’s Barboza, Swan Dive in Portland, Lilypad in Cambridge, Baby’s All Right in Brooklyn and Philadelphia’s Nikki Lopez before wrapping up Nov. 7 at The Pinhook in Durham.

The indie pop band will be supported by Kids with a Crime Spree on select dates. The two shows at San Francisco’s Castro Theatre on Aug. 22 and 23 are sold out and will feature The Breeders as a guest.

For tickets, visit Imperial Teen’s [Bandsintown page](https://www.bandsintown.com/a/12644-imperial-teen).
