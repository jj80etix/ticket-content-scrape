# Tinashe Announces ‘Popstar’-Focused Shows in Chicago, NY, & LA

## Summary

Tinashe announced three shows in Chicago, Brooklyn, and Los Angeles this fall to support her upcoming album "Popstar," marking the first live performances of the album in full. Pre-sale begins July 22 with public on-sale July 24.

### Key Points
- Three shows announced: Oct. 18 Chicago (The Salt Shed), Oct. 24 Brooklyn (Brooklyn Paramount), Nov. 7 Los Angeles (Hollywood Palladium).
- "Popstar" will be Tinashe's eighth studio album.
- VIP packages include a DJ after-party in addition to show tickets.
- Follows 2024's "Quantum Baby" and the "Match My Freak" World Tour, which included 48 shows.
- Pre-sale begins July 22 at 10 a.m. local time; public on-sale July 24.

Tinashe, the multi-platinum-selling singer, songwriter, producer and DJ, has announced three shows in Chicago, Brooklyn and Los Angeles this fall in support of her upcoming album, *Popstar*. This trio of shows will see Tinashe perform the album live for the first time.

Pre-sale begins on July 22 at 10 a.m. local time, with public on-sale this July 24 at 10 a.m. local time. VIP packages will include tickets to the show and access to a DJ after-party. More ticket information can be found [here](https://www.tinashenow.com/).

*Popstar* will be Tinashe’s eighth studio LP, launching her into a new era that is “filled with defiant and unapologetic anthems, paired with high-energy, choreography-filled visuals to match that continue to show off her innate ability to flawlessly blend genres and pave her own path,” per the official press release for the LP. The album follows 2024’s *Quantum Baby,* which yielded viral singles, “Nasty,” “No Broke Boys,” and “Getting No Sleep,” and was supported by the “Match My Freak: World Tour.” The “Match My Freak: World Tour” included 48 shows with a slew of sold-out dates such as the Nov. 1 stop at The Anthem in Washington, D.C., grossing $152,874, per [Pollstar Boxoffice reports.](https://pollstar.com/tour-histories/artists/138763)

Oct. 18 – Chicago, Illinois – The Salt Shed

Oct. 24 – Brooklyn, NY – Brooklyn Paramount

Nov. 7 – Los Angeles, CA – Hollywood Palladium
