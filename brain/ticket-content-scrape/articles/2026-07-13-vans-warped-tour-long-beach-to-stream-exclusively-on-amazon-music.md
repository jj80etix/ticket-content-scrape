# Vans Warped Tour Long Beach to Stream Exclusively on Amazon Music

## Summary

Amazon Music will exclusively livestream the Vans Warped Tour's Long Beach stop (July 25-26) across its app, Twitch, Prime Video, and Fire TV/Samsung TV Plus channels. The two-day punk/alternative/rock festival features over 100 artists including Jimmy Eat World, Taking Back Sunday, and Third Eye Blind, with hosted segments and artist interviews throughout the weekend.

### Key Points
- Amazon Music holds exclusive livestream rights to the Long Beach Vans Warped Tour stop, July 25-26.
- Available via Amazon Music app, Twitch, Prime Video, and Fire TV/Samsung TV Plus.
- Lineup includes Jimmy Eat World, Taking Back Sunday, Simple Plan, Third Eye Blind, Bowling for Soup.
- Produced in partnership with Insomniac; includes skateboarding/action-sports appearances alongside music.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Vans-Warped-Tour-.webp)

Amazon Music will exclusively livestream the Vans Warped Tour from Long Beach later this month, allowing fans to watch performances from more than 100 artists across the two-day festival.

The event is scheduled for July 25 to 26 at the Downtown Long Beach Shoreline Waterfront. This year’s lineup includes Jimmy Eat World, Taking Back Sunday, Simple Plan, Third Eye Blind, Bowling for Soup and more.

The livestream will be available on the Amazon Music app, Amazon Music’s channels on Twitch and Prime Video, and the Amazon Live Channel for viewers using Fire TV and Samsung TV Plus.

The Long Beach festival is produced in partnership with Insomniac and continues the Vans Warped Tour’s legacy of showcasing punk, alternative and rock artists. In addition to the musical performances, the event will feature appearances from professional skateboarders and action sports athletes.

Amazon Music’s livestream will be hosted by rock musician Lilith Czar, also known as Juliet Simms, SiriusXM host Caity Babs and Black Veil Brides frontman Andy Biersack. Throughout the weekend, the hosts will interview artists performing at the festival and preview several of the scheduled performances. Fans can also listen to the official 2026 Vans Warped Tour playlist on Amazon Music before the festival begins.

The Long Beach event is one of four Vans Warped Tour stops scheduled for 2026. Following the July festival in California, the tour will head to Orlando, Florida, in November. It will also expand internationally with inaugural festivals in Montreal in August and Mexico City in September.
