# Young Thug Announces The New Generation Tour With U.S. and European Dates

## Summary

Young Thug has announced The New Generation Tour, his first headlining run since 2019, spanning U.S. and European dates from September 1 to October 24, 2026. Rapper NAV will support on all U.S. dates. Presales begin July 14-15 via Spotify and artist channels, with general onsale July 17.

### Key Points
- Tour opens September 1 in Rogers, AR (Walmart AMP) and closes the North American leg October 4 in Inglewood, CA (YouTube Theatre).
- European leg runs October 14-24 with stops in Amsterdam, Dusseldorf, Lodz, and Paris.
- NAV joins as special guest for all U.S. shows.
- Spotify Reserved presale July 14, artist presale July 15, general onsale July 17.


## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/New-Generation-Young-Thug.jpg)

Young Thug has announced The New Generation Tour, a U.S. and European tour, marking the rapper’s first headlining run since 2019.

The run is scheduled to begin on September 1 in Rogers, AR, at Walmart AMP. From there, the tour is set to make stops in cities such as Minneapolis, Chicago, Boston, New York, Washington D.C., Atlanta and Austin before wrapping up the North American leg on October 4 in Inglewood, CA, at YouTube Theatre.

Notably, Young Thug will perform at Michigan Lottery Amphitheatre on Tuesday, September 8. Rapper NAV will join Young Thug as a special guest for all U.S. dates on the tour.

Following the U.S. shows, Young Thug will take The New Generation Tour to Europe, with his first overseas performances scheduled for October 14 in Amsterdam at AFAS Live. From there, the rapper will make stops in Dusseldorf and Lodz before his final show on October 24 in Paris at Adidas Arena.

Fans will have access to presales beginning with the Spotify Reserved presale on Tuesday, July 14, at 12 p.m. local time. An artist presale will follow on Wednesday, July 15, at 10 a.m. local time, with additional presale opportunities available throughout the week.

Tickets for The New Generation Tour will go on sale to the general public on Friday, July 17, at 10 a.m. local time.
