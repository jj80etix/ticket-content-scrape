# Colorado Symphony, Cole Swindell and Chris Young Lead This Week’s Hottest Concert Tickets

## Summary

TicketNews' weekly "hottest concert tickets" roundup, based on MEGASeats sales data for events between July 15–22, highlights the Colorado Symphony Orchestra's film-score program at Red Rocks and a wave of country headliners (Cole Swindell, Chris Young, Dierks Bentley, Chris Stapleton, Morgan Wallen) topping this week's national sell-through rankings. Pacific Amphitheatre's three-night country run (Midland, Young, Swindell) captures three of the week's eleven strongest sellers.

### Key Points
- Colorado Symphony's "Music of Hans Zimmer & John Williams" at Red Rocks (July 21) tops the list at an avg. $93/ticket.
- Country acts occupy over half of the top-20 chart; Pacific Amphitheatre (Costa Mesa) hosts Midland (7/17), Chris Young (7/18) and Cole Swindell (7/19) on consecutive nights.
- Morgan Wallen's four-act Baltimore stadium show (M&T Bank Stadium, 7/18) has the week's highest avg. price at $317.
- Olivia Dean at Crypto.com Arena (7/15) posts the second-highest avg. price at $376.

A film-score program at Red Rocks and a deep lineup of country concerts are shaping the live-event calendar this week. Recent MEGASeats sales data for events scheduled from July 15 through July 22 places the [Colorado Symphony Orchestra](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/colorado-symphony-orchestra-8178?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) ’s performance of music by Hans Zimmer and John Williams at the top of the list, followed by [Cole Swindell](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/cole-swindell-60334?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) and [Chris Young](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/chris-young-10161?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10).

Concerts occupy every position in the week’s top 20, with country headliners and country-centered package shows accounting for more than half of the list. The concentration is especially noticeable at Pacific Amphitheatre in Costa Mesa, where Midland performs July 17, Young follows July 18 and Swindell closes the three-night run July 19. All three shows rank among the week’s 11 strongest sellers.

Other country standouts include [Dierks Bentley](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/dierks-bentley-312?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) sharing a St. Augustine bill with Ricky Skaggs and Cole Goodwin, [Chris Stapleton](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/chris-stapleton-68538?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) bringing his All-American Road Show with Grace Potter to Portland, and [Morgan Wallen](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/morgan-wallen-107605?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) leading a four-act stadium lineup in Baltimore. Tracy Lawrence, Willie Nelson & Family, Aaron Lewis and Jason Aldean add to the genre’s strong showing.

Multi-act packages are also helping shape the week beyond country music. [Lionel Richie](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/lionel-richie-1881?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) and [Earth, Wind & Fire](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/earth-wind-and-fire-1493?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) bring a pairing of major hit catalogs to Tampa, while [Stone Temple Pilots](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/stone-temple-pilots-976?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews&promoCode=TICKETNEWS10) and Local H represent the week’s alternative-rock offerings. Mötley Crüe with Tesla, The Black Crowes with Whiskey Myers and Southall, and an Evening of Funk featuring Confunkshun, The Ohio Players and The Original Lakeside give fans several more opportunities to see multiple recognizable acts on one ticket.
