# Touring Entertainment Report 2026

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's promotional table-of-contents page for its "Touring Entertainment Report 2026," listing feature articles and cover topics rather than presenting original reporting itself. Teased sections cover Hans Zimmer's live-scoring tour, personalization trends in touring exhibitions, LatAm touring opportunities, exhibition trends beyond immersive formats, and financial strategies for smaller ("beyond AAA") markets.

### Key Points
- Cover features include Hans Zimmer's "The Next Level" tour, touring exhibitions racing to deliver hyper-personalized experiences, and LatAm as a touring growth market.
- Additional linked articles cover live-orchestra/cinema concert experiences, trusted-IP exhibition trends, and financial-success strategies for smaller markets ("Beyond AAA Markets").
- Includes several executive Q&As (e.g., Lightroom CEO Richard Slaney) and opinion pieces, including one on AI's impact on the arts.
- Page is a marketing/contents listing linking out to individual magazine articles rather than containing substantive reporting itself; no ticketing-platform, pricing, regulatory, or technology content appears in the page body.
