# Jon Pardi’s ‘Gamblin’ Man Tour’ Rolls Out This Fall

## Summary

Country singer Jon Pardi has announced his "Gamblin' Man Tour," a 19-date run through U.S. arenas and amphitheaters this fall. The tour opens September 17 in Richmond, Virginia and closes November 14 in Independence, Missouri, with support from Alexandra Kay and Sadie Bass. Presales begin July 21, ahead of general on-sale July 24.

### Key Points
- 19 dates across the South, Midwest, and West, running September 17 – November 14, 2026.
- Support acts: Alexandra Kay and Sadie Bass.
- Presale begins 10 a.m. local time July 21; general on-sale begins 10 a.m. local time July 24.
- Cited recent Pollstar Boxoffice data: $486,559 gross on 6,225 tickets at Lafayette, LA's Cajundome (March 21).
- Pardi is represented by WME.

Honky-tonkin’ neo-traditional revivalist Jon Pardi will visit 19 arenas and amphitheaters across the U.S. this fall on his “Gamblin’ Man Tour.”

The run kicks off Sept. 17 at Richmond, Virginia’s Allianz Amphitheater at Riverfront and visits markets across the South, Midwest and West. The tour wraps Nov. 14 in Independence, Missouri at Cable Dahmer Arena.

Alexandra Kay and Sadie Bass will support.

Presales begin at 10 a.m. local July 21 with general on-sale at 10 a.m. local July 24.

Recent Pollstar Boxoffice highlights for Pardi include a $486,559 gross on 6,225 tickets at Lafayette, Louisiana’s Cajundome March 21.

Pardi is repped by WME.
