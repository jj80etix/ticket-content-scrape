# Issa Rae, Prentice Penny Embarking On ‘Insecure: 10th Anniversary Tour’

## Summary

Issa Rae and Prentice Penny are launching a 13-city U.S. theater tour this fall celebrating the 10th anniversary of Insecure, promoted by Live Nation. The tour kicks off September 10 in Philadelphia and wraps October 8 in Inglewood, California, with guest appearances from several show cast members on select dates. Citi cardmember and general presales run this week ahead of a July 16 general onsale.

### Key Points
- 13-city tour promoted by Live Nation runs Sept. 10 (Philadelphia) through Oct. 8 (Inglewood, CA).
- Special guest appearances by Yvonne Orji, Jay Ellis, and Natasha Rothwell on select dates.
- Citi presale begins July 15; general onsale begins July 16 via LiveNation.com.

Ten years after premiering her hit series *Insecure*, writer, producer and actress Issa Rae is bringing some nostalgia to U.S. theaters this fall and celebrating the show’s anniversary alongside showrunner Prentice Penny.

The 13-city tour promoted by Live Nation kicks off Sept. 10 at The Met Philadelphia presented by Highmark. The routing includes stops at The Fillmore Detroit, Boston’s MGM Music Hall at Fenway, Brooklyn Paramount in New York, Palazzo Theatre in Las Vegas, Chicago Theatre and Houston’s Bayou Music Center before wrapping up Oct. 8 at YouTube Theater in Inglewood, California.

Rae and Penny recently celebrated the show’s anniversary by launching their podcast, BLOCC PARTY: AN INSECURE PODCAST, and the duo is ready to bring the conversation to U.S. stages along with special guest appearances by Yvonne Orji, Jay Ellis and Natasha Rothwell on select dates.

Citi cardmembers have access to a presale beginning July 15 at noon local time, and additional presales will run throughout the week leading up to the general onsale, which begins July 16 at noon local time. Visit LiveNation.com for more information.
