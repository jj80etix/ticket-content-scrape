# WWE Adds 14 Fall Dates for Raw, SmackDown, Worlds Collide

## Summary

WWE announced 14 newly confirmed fall U.S. dates for Raw, SmackDown, and a special Worlds Collide crossover event with Lucha Libre AAA, running September 18 through November 2, 2026. Tickets for all 14 events go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time, with an exclusive presale beginning July 23.

### Key Points
- Run spans September 18 (SmackDown, Corpus Christi, TX) through November 2 (Raw, Barclays Center, Brooklyn, NY).
- Worlds Collide event on September 26 at Allstate Arena near Chicago pairs WWE talent with Lucha Libre AAA Superstars.
- General on-sale is Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time; an exclusive presale starts Thursday, July 23.
- 14 total dates across venues in Texas, Indiana, Illinois, Wisconsin, Colorado, Missouri, Louisiana, California, West Virginia, and New York.
- Tickets and event details are handled through WWE's official events page.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Untitled-design-2024-06-24T133036.205.webp)

WWE is loading up its fall calendar with 14 newly announced U.S. dates featuring Raw, SmackDown and a special Worlds Collide event.

The run begins Sept. 18 with SmackDown at the Hilliard Center in Corpus Christi, Texas, and continues through Nov. 2, when Raw takes over Brooklyn’s Barclays Center.

One of the biggest additions is Worlds Collide on Sept. 26 at Allstate Arena near Chicago. The live event will bring together WWE talent and Superstars from Lucha Libre AAA.

Tickets for all 14 events go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time [here](https://www.wwe.com/events/results/all-events/all-dates/40.740/-74.000/new%20york,%20NY,%20USA/US). An exclusive presale begins Thursday, July 23.

Tickets and additional event information are available through WWE’s [official events page.](https://www.wwe.com/events/results/all-events/all-dates/40.740/-74.000/new%20york,%20NY,%20USA/US)
