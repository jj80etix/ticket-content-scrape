# Valentina to Make Broadway Debut in ‘The Rocky Horror Show’ Revival

## Summary

Drag Race alum Valentina joins the Broadway revival of "The Rocky Horror Show" as Columbia, beginning performances July 21, taking over from Michaela Jaé Rodriguez. The production, directed by Sam Pinkleton and led by Luke Evans and Rachel Dratch, has earned nine 2026 Tony nominations.

### Key Points
- Valentina begins performances July 21 as Columbia.
- She replaces Michaela Jaé Rodriguez, with understudies covering the gap.
- The revival has nine 2026 Tony nominations, including Best Revival of a Musical.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/The-Rocky-Horror-Show-1.webp)

Valentina, known for competing on “RuPaul’s Drag Race” is set to make her Broadway debut in the current revival of “The Rocky Horror Show.” The performer will join the cast as Columbia, beginning performances on July 21.

Valentina takes over the role after the departure of Golden Globe winner Michaela Jaé Rodriguez earlier this summer. Since Rodriguez’s exit, understudies Caleb Quezon and Larkin Reilly have shared performances as Columbia until Valentina’s arrival.

The Broadway revival is directed by Tony Award winner Sam Pinkleton, whose recent credits include “Oh, Mary!” The production features an ensemble led by Luke Evans as Dr. Frank-N-Furter and Rachel Dratch as the Narrator.

The principal cast also includes Andrew Durand as Brad, Stephanie Hsu as Janet, Amber Gray as Riff Raff, Harvey Guillén in the dual role of Eddie and Dr. Scott, and Josh Rivera as Rocky. Additional cast members include Renée Albulario, Anania, Boy Radio, Caleb Quezon, Andres Quintero, Larkin Reilly, Paul Soileau and John Yi.
