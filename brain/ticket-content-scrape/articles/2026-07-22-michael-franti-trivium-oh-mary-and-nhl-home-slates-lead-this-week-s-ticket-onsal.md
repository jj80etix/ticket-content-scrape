# Michael Franti, Trivium, Oh, Mary! and NHL Home Slates Lead This Week’s Ticket Onsales

## Summary

This week's ticket onsale calendar is led by Michael Franti, Trivium, "Oh, Mary!", and large home-game ticket batches for the Buffalo Sabres and New York Islanders. Onsales are concentrated late in the week, with Friday carrying the largest volume across concerts, sports, theater, comedy, and seasonal programming through spring 2027.

### Key Points
- Michael Franti: 20+ fall shows on sale, late September through October.
- Trivium: ~20-market hard-rock/metal tour on sale for November-December.
- "Oh, Mary!" adds 30+ performances at the Ahmanson Theatre in Los Angeles (November-early December).
- Jon Pardi and Rebecca Black also have broad fall tour on-sales this week.
- NHL home-game ticket batches for the Sabres and Islanders lead the sports onsale volume.

## Sabres and Islanders Release Large Home-Game Batches

Professional hockey supplies the week’s largest sports component. Tickets for more than 40 [Buffalo Sabres](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/buffalo-sabres-179?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews) games at [KeyBank Center](https://www.megaseats.com/venues/keybank-center-98?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews) are included, covering preseason play through the end of the regular season next spring.

The [New York Islanders](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/new-york-islanders-730?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews) also have a large group of home games coming on sale at [UBS Arena](https://www.megaseats.com/venues/ubs-arena-35897?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews), with opponents ranging across both conferences and dates extending from October into April.

Elsewhere in sports and arena entertainment, this week’s releases include select Boston Celtics preseason games, San Diego State and Boise State football home dates, and new events for [WWE Raw](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/wwe-raw-42285?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews) and [WWE SmackDown](https://www.megaseats.com/performers/wwe-smackdown-42885?utm_medium=content&utm_source=ticketnews&utm_campaign=ticketnews).
