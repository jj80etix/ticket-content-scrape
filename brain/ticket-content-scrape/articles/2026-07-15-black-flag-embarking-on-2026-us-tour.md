# Black Flag Embarking On 2026 US Tour

## Summary

Punk band Black Flag announced a 45-city 2026 U.S. tour running September 3 through November 1, kicking off at Belly Up in Aspen, CO, and wrapping at House of Blues in Houston, TX. The lineup features founding guitarist Greg Ginn alongside vocalist Max Zanelly, bassist David Rodriguez, and drummer Bryce Weston. The band also teased additional 2027 dates.

### Key Points
- 45 U.S. tour dates from Sept. 3, 2026 (Belly Up, Aspen, CO) through Nov. 1, 2026 (House of Blues, Houston, TX).
- Lineup: Greg Ginn (guitar), Max Zanelly (vocals), David Rodriguez (bass), Bryce Weston (drums).
- Routing includes stops in Fargo, Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, Virginia Beach, Tulsa, Redondo Beach, Napa, and Dallas.
- Band announced additional 2027 tour dates to come.
- Booked by Artist Worldwide Agency; ticket info via blackflagband.com.

American punk band Black Flag is hitting the road later this year, performing in 45 cities across the country from September through early November.

Founding guitarist Greg Ginn will be joined by lead vocalist Max Zanelly, bassist David Rodriguez and drummer Bryce Weston. The quartet kick off their 2026 tour Sept. 3 at Belly Up in Aspen, and the routing includes stops at Fargo’s The Aquarium, Pittsburgh’s Crafthouse, Nikki Lopez in Philadelphia, Virginia Beach’s Elevation 27, The Shrine in Tulsa, Redondo Beach’s Deep End Dive, Napa Music Hall in Northern California and AM/FM in Dallas before wrapping up Nov. 1 at House of Blues in Houston.

The band also announced more shows are coming in 2027. Visit [blackflagband.com](https://www.blackflagband.com/) for information about tickets.

Black Flag is booked by Artist Worldwide Agency.
