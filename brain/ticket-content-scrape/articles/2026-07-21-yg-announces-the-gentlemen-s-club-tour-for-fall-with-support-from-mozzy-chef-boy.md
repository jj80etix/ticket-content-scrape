# YG Announces ‘The Gentlemen’s Club Tour’ For Fall With Support From Mozzy, Chef Boy, Kalan.FrFr

## Summary

West Coast rapper YG announced the 19-city "Gentlemen's Club Tour" for fall 2026, running Boston to Honolulu with support from Mozzy, Chef Boy, and Kalan.FrFr on select dates.

### Key Points
- Tour runs Sept. 10–Nov. 7 across East Coast clubs and West Coast arenas.
- General on-sale July 24; artist presale/VIP July 22.
- YG faces The Game in an Apple Music/Complex-presented Verzuz battle July 23.

Influential West Coast rapper YG will hit the road in the fall to bring “The Gentlemen’s Club Tour” to both coasts, including with arenas on the West Coast into early November.

In support of YG’s latest album, *THE GENTLEMEN’S CLUB*, which is available now, the 19-city tour will feature support from fellow West Coast rappers Mozzy and Chef Boy, as well as Kalan.FrFr and Natalie Nunn on select dates. Dates kick off Sept. 10 in Boston and runs through the Fall, before wrapping on November 7 in Honolulu, Hawaii. Dates include West Coast arenas including multiple SoCal stops including Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles, Viejas Arena in San Diego and, a little farther north, Oakland Arena and Save Mart Center in Fresno.

General ticket onsale begins Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time. Artist Presale and VIP Packages start July 22 at 10 a.m.

Later this week, YG will face off against The Game in a West Coast Verzuz battle airing Thursday, July 23 at 6:30 p.m. PT, presented by Apple Music and Complex.

Recent boxoffice reports from YG include a sold-out Kia Forum gig in 2023, which sold 13,850 tickets and grossed $850,000. In fall 2024 he performed an extensive run of large U.S. clubs including multiple House of Blues and Fillmore venues.

YG is represented by UTA.
