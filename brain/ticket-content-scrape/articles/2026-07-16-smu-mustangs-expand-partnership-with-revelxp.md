# SMU Mustangs expand partnership with Revelxp

## Summary

SMU Mustangs (Southern Methodist University athletics) have extended their partnership with hospitality provider Revelxp, building on Revelxp's existing role as SMU's official gameday hospitality and operations partner. As part of the extension, Mustang Partners and Revelxp are launching "Revelxp Reserve," giving Mustang Club donors access to private suites, premium seating, club/lounge access, all-inclusive food and beverage, and VIP pregame/postgame experiences.

### Key Points
- SMU Mustangs extended their strategic partnership with hospitality provider Revelxp.
- Revelxp remains SMU's official gameday hospitality and operations partner.
- New offering: "Revelxp Reserve," available to SMU Mustang Club donors.
- Revelxp Reserve includes private suites, premium seating, club/lounge access, all-inclusive food and beverage, and VIP pregame/postgame experiences.
- Quotes from Lauren Adee (SMU deputy director of athletics) and Wes Day (Revelxp CEO) frame the deal around revenue generation and fan-experience enhancement tied to SMU's "Boulevard" gameday tradition.

## Transcript

TheTicketingBusiness News

**The Southern Methodist University (SMU) Mustangs have extended their strategic partnership with hospitality provider Revelxp.**

The new agreement aims to build upon Revelxp’s existing role as SMU’s official gameday hospitality and operations partner while looking to enhance the fan experience on campus, generating new revenue, and improving operational efficiencies.

“This extension is another important step as we continue to generate impactful revenue-generating partnerships for SMU Athletics,” said Lauren Adee, SMU deputy director of athletics.

“The Boulevard and our gameday experience are such a big part of what makes SMU special, and together they’ve become one of the most unique traditions in college football.

“Revelxp has been a great partner, and this extension allows us to keep enhancing revenue generation opportunities and our fan experience.”

As part of the expanded partnership, Mustang Partners and Revelxp are launching Revelxp Reserve, offering SMU Mustang Club donors access to curated hospitality experiences including private suites and premium seating, club and lounge access, all-inclusive food and beverage, and VIP pregame and postgame experiences.

“For Revelxp, our partnerships are the cornerstone of our business, so we are grateful and honoured to continue working alongside the SMU brand and team,” said Revelxp CEO Wes Day.

“The Boulevard and SMU’s collective gameday experience has continued to grow and create contagious energy that is exciting to be a part of.

“We look forward to further supporting this phenomenal tradition with unsurpassed service for many years to come.”
