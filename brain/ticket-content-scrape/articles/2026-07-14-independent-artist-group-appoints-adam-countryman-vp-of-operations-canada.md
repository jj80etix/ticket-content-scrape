# Independent Artist Group Appoints Adam Countryman VP Of Operations, Canada

## Summary

Independent Artist Group has promoted Adam Countryman to Vice President of Operations, Canada, where he will lead the agency's growth in the country while supporting global artist development chief Nick Storch. Countryman, a 15-year industry veteran previously at The Agency Group, UTA and Paradigm, joined IAG in 2020.

### Key Points
- Adam Countryman promoted to VP of Operations, Canada at Independent Artist Group.
- He works alongside EVP of Global Artist Development Nick Storch on artists including Sleep Token, Ghost, Spiritbox, Volbeat and Anthrax.
- Countryman has prior experience at The Agency Group, UTA and Paradigm; joined IAG in 2020.

Independent Artist Group has promoted Adam Countryman to Vice President of Operations, Canada. The role will see Countryman spearheading the agency’s “continued growth and investment in the country while continuing to work closely to help manage the global business of IAG Executive Vice-President of Global Artist Development Nick Storch.”

Countryman, who is based in Toronto, has more than 15 years of experience in the business, including working for The Agency Group, UTA, and Paradigm. He joined IAG in 2020 and works with Stroch on artists including Sleep Token, Ghost And Spiritbox, Volbeat, Gorija, Anthrax And Slayer, among many others.

“Adam has played a significant role helping the agency and me personally scale our business and significantly strengthen Independent Artist Group’s presence in Canada and Internationally,” said Storch in a statement. “He is a hard worker, detail oriented, with a keen strategic mind that has proven to be invaluable to us.”
