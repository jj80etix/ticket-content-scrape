# Rebecca Black to Bring ‘Miss Delicious’ Tour Across North America This Fall

## Summary

Pop artist Rebecca Black has announced "Exhibitionism on Tour – Rebecca Black as: Miss Delicious," a 27-date North American headlining run beginning September 23 in Boston and wrapping November 5 in Los Angeles. The tour includes festival stops at All Things Go NYC/DC, Austin City Limits, and For You Festival, alongside a multi-stage presale schedule ahead of general on-sale.

### Key Points
- 27 dates across the U.S. and Canada, September 23 – November 5, 2026, opening in Boston and closing in Los Angeles.
- Festival appearances: All Things Go NYC (9/25), All Things Go DC (9/26), Austin City Limits (10/2), For You Festival (11/15).
- Artist presale begins July 22 at 10 a.m. local time; local presales follow July 23 at 10 a.m. local time; both end July 23 at 11:59 p.m. local time.
- General public on-sale begins July 24 at 10 a.m. local time.
- Additional details available via rebeccablack.co.

Rebecca Black has announced plans for a new headlining tour across North America this fall.

The Mexican-American pop artist will embark on “Exhibitionism on Tour – Rebecca Black as: Miss Delicious,” a 27-date run featuring performances in cities throughout the United States and Canada. The tour is scheduled to begin on September 23 at Royale in Boston, Massachusetts.
