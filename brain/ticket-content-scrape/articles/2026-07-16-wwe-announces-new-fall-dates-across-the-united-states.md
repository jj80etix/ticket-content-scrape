# WWE Announces New Fall Dates Across the United States

## Summary

WWE has added 14 new fall tour stops across the United States, spanning Raw, SmackDown, and a "Worlds Collide" live event that will feature newly acquired AAA/Lucha Libre talent alongside WWE's roster. The tour runs from September 18 in Corpus Christi, TX through November 2 in Brooklyn, NY. Tickets go on general sale Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time, with an exclusive presale starting July 23.

### Key Points
- 14 new stops added, running September 18 (SmackDown, Corpus Christi, TX) through November 2 (Raw, Barclays Center, Brooklyn, NY).
- Worlds Collide takes place September 26 at Allstate Arena in Chicago, IL, combining WWE with the newly acquired AAA and its Lucha Libre Superstars.
- General on-sale begins Friday, July 24 at 10 a.m. local time; presale opens July 23.
- Additional stops include Frost Bank Center (San Antonio), Gainbridge Fieldhouse (Indianapolis), Fiserv Forum (Milwaukee), Ball Arena (Denver), and Golden 1 Center (Sacramento).
- Tickets and full event details are available at WWE.com/Events.

The WWE has added brand-new fall dates, touring the United States with 14 new stops. The new tour will include Raw, Smackdown, and Worlds Collide – a live event set to include WWE and the newly acquired AAA and its Lucha Libre Superstars. As a part of the tour, Worlds Collide will go down in Chicago, IL, at Allstate Arena on Sept. 26. The flurry of new dates begins on Sept. 19 at Corpus Christi, TX at the Hilliard Center, with stops in San Antonio, TX, Denver, CO, and Sacramento, CA, with the new dates ending in Brooklyn, New York at the Barclays Center. See the full tour routing below.

Tickets for each event will go on sale Friday, July 24, at 10 a.m. local time. Fans can purchase individual event tickets during an exclusive presale starting July 23. Tickets and event information can also be found [here](http://WWE.com/Events).

September 18 – WWE SmackDown @ the Hilliard Center in Corpus Christi, Texas

September 21 – WWE Raw @ the Frost Bank Center in San Antonio, Texas

September 25 – WWE Smackdown @ the Gainbridge Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, Indiana

September 26 – Worlds Collide @ the Allstate Arena in Chicago, Illinois

September 28 – WWE Raw @ Fiserv Forum in Milwaukee, Wisconsin

October 2 – WWE SmackDown @ Ball Arena in Denver, Colorado
