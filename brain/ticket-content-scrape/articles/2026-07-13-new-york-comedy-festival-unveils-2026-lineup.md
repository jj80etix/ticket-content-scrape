# New York Comedy Festival Unveils 2026 Lineup

## Summary

The 2026 New York Comedy Festival unveiled its lineup for its November 6-15 run, featuring over 200 comedians and 100+ shows across five boroughs at venues including Carnegie Hall, the Beacon Theatre, and Town Hall.

### Key Points
- Runs November 6-15, 2026, across five NYC boroughs.
- 200+ comedians, 100+ shows including stand-up, live podcasts, screenings, and panels.
- Headliners include Tim Dillon, Marc Maron, Ilana Glazer, Ziwe, and Jordan Klepper.
- Founded in 2004 by Caroline Hirsch; billed as the largest/longest-running comedy festival in the U.S.

Details are here for the 2026 New York Comedy Festival, featuring more than 200 comedians and over 100 shows in five boroughs over 10 days.

The event, which is touted as the “the largest and longest-running annual comedy festival in the United States,” returns Nov. 6-15. Shows will take place in some of New York’s most iconic venues including Carnegie Hall, the Beacon Theatre, Town Hall and Kings Theatre, along with countless clubs.

NYCF features headliners including Ben Bankas, Mojo Brookzz, Comic Relief x The Moth, Tim Dillon, Dropout, Adam Friedland, Gayme Show! Starring Matt Rogers and Dave Mizzoni, Ilana Glazer, Jess Hilarious, Jordan Jensen, Jordan Klepper, Marc Maron, Joanne McNally, Ms. Pat, Sarah Sherman & Patti Harrison, Daniel Sloss, Ziwe and more, with additional shows to be announced soon.

Today’s announcement notes that since the comedy festival was founded by Caroline Hirsch in 2004, the event has “championed every corner of the comedy world with a bold, diverse lineup that extends far beyond stand-up. From live podcast recordings, film and television screenings, and sketch and improv showcases to thought-provoking conversations, industry panels and star-studded fundraising events, the festival celebrates comedy in all its forms.”

In a statement Hirsch said: “Each year, the New York Comedy Festival serves as both a celebration of comedy’s biggest voices and a showcase for the talent shaping its future. This year’s lineup brings together established stars, fan favorites, and a remarkable new generation of comedians. We’re very proud to continue making New York City the epicenter of the comedy world, where artists, industry leaders, and fans come together to experience the very best the art form has to offer.”

A ticket presale runs from July 15 at 11 a.m. ET through July 19 at 10 p.m. ET. The general public onsale starts July 20 at 11 a.m. ET. Fans can follow New York Comedy Festival on Instagram and sign up for its email newsletter to get the presale code.

Visit [https://nycomedyfestival.com/](https://nycomedyfestival.com/) for more information.
