# Rockettes Expand 2026 Christmas Spectacular With New Number, Record 230 Shows

## Summary

The Radio City Rockettes' 2026 Christmas Spectacular will expand to a record 230 performances (Nov. 4, 2026–Jan. 4, 2027), adding a new production number and new immersive technology. This follows a record-breaking 2025 season that celebrated the Rockettes' 100th anniversary and drew over 1.2 million guests.

### Key Points
- The 2026 run adds a new Rockettes number, bringing the show to 10 total production numbers, while keeping staples like "Parade of the Wooden Soldiers" and "12 Days of Christmas."
- A record 230 performances is the most in the show's history since its 1933 debut.
- The 2025 100th-anniversary season drew 1.2 million-plus guests over 216 shows, the highest attendance in 25 years.
- New immersive technology builds on 2025 additions including digital projections, holograms, fairy drones and Sphere Immersive Sound.
- Tickets start at $50 at the Radio City box office and $55 online (including service charges); group sales are available for parties of nine or more.
- The production has welcomed more than 73 million guests total since opening in 1933.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/rockettes.jpg)

The Radio City Rockettes are making the Christmas Spectacular bigger than ever for 2026.

The holiday production will return to Radio City Music Hall from Nov. 4, 2026, through Jan. 4, 2027, with a brand-new Rockettes number, new immersive technology and a record 230 performances — the most in the show’s history since its 1933 debut.

Tickets are on sale now through the Rockettes’ [website](https://www.rockettes.com/Christmas/).

The expanded run follows a record-breaking 2025 season that celebrated the Rockettes’ 100th anniversary. More than 1.2 million guests attended 216 performances, marking the production’s highest attendance in 25 years.
