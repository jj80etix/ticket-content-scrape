# Superstruct UK Appoints Jacqui Harris As Managing Director

## Summary

Superstruct Entertainment has appointed Jacqui Harris as Managing Director of its UK division. Harris joins from nearly two decades at AEG Presents UK, most recently as VP and General Manager of the UK Touring division, where she worked on major events including BST Hyde Park, All Points East, and Eden Sessions Cornwall.

### Key Points
- Jacqui Harris named Managing Director, Superstruct Entertainment UK.
- She spent almost 20 years at AEG Presents UK, most recently as VP/GM of UK Touring.
- Prior work included BST Hyde Park, All Points East, and Eden Sessions Cornwall.
- She led UK Touring operations across stadium, arena and greenfield events for major artists.

Superstruct announced the appointment of Jacqui Harris as Managing Director of Superstruct Entertainment UK.

Harris joins Superstruct Entertainment following almost two decades at AEG Presents UK, one of the country’s leading live music promoters, where she most recently served as Vice President and General Manager for the UK Touring division.

During her time at AEG, her work spanned major outdoor events, including All Points East, Eden Sessions Cornwall and the award-winning American Express presents BST Hyde Park.

At BST Hyde Park, AEG Presents flagship event in the UK, Harris played a key role in driving commercial growth and broadening the event’s reach. She also led the team delivering UK Touring operations across stadium, arena and greenfield events for some of the world’s biggest artists.

She spent a total 18 years with AEG Presents UK. Prior to that, she held early-career roles across Festival Republic’s festival portfolio. Harris was recently recognized as one of the top 50 international female executives through the LSE Global Leaders Scholarship with Santander W50 2026.
