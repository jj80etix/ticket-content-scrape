# Tiësto, Chris Lake, Subtronics Headlining Dallas’ Lights All Night

## Summary

Dallas' Lights All Night electronic music festival has announced its 16th-anniversary lineup, headlined by Tiësto, Chris Lake and Subtronics at Fair Park. The two-day New Year's Eve event has drawn more than 300,000 fans since debuting in 2010 and has previously featured artists like Calvin Harris, Deadmau5 and Skrillex. General on-sale begins July 9 following a presale last week.

### Key Points
- 16th edition of Lights All Night takes place at Fair Park, Dallas, headlined by Tiësto, Chris Lake, and Subtronics.
- Produced by Disco Presents (founder/CEO James "Disco Donnie" Estopinal Jr.).
- Full lineup includes ~35 additional acts (ARMNHMR, Gryffin, I Hate Models, Kettama, Ninajirachi, and others).
- General on-sale opens July 9 at 10 a.m. CT via LightsAllNight.com, following a bundled presale the prior week.
- Festival has hosted 300,000+ fans since its 2010 debut and has featured past headliners including Calvin Harris, Deadmau5, and Skrillex.

Texas’ longest-running New Year’s Eve electronic music festival has unveiled its lineup for this year’s edition taking place at the Fair Park in Dallas. Lights All Night is celebrating its 16 <sup>th</sup> anniversary with Tiësto, Chris Lake and Subtronics headlining the popular two-day event.

Fans can expect an immersive production and a memorable midnight countdown on a 277-acre National Historic Landmark.

“For 16 years, Lights All Night has continued to grow because of the fans who have made it their New Year’s Eve tradition,” James “Disco Donnie” Estopinal Jr., founder and CEO of Disco Presents, said in a statement. “Returning to Fair Park feels like the right next step in the festival’s evolution. It’s where some of our earliest memories were made, but this year’s event is all about looking forward. We’re building the biggest Lights All Night yet, and we can’t wait to celebrate the countdown into 2027 with everyone.”

Tickets for the event go on sale July 9 at 10 a.m. CT. With a successful bind presale last week, fans are encouraged to secure passes early before prices increase. Visit [LightsAllNight.com](https://lightsallnight.com/) for more information.

Lights All Night debuted on New Year’s Eve in 2010 and grew from a single-night celebration into one of North America’s top electronic music festivals to ring in the new year. The event has welcomed more than 300,000 fans and featured artists like Calvin Harris, Deadmau5, Skrillex, The Chainsmokers, Excision, GRiZ, Illenium and many more.
