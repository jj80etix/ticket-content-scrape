# Sunny Hill Festival Names Fever Official International Ticketing Partner for 2026

## Summary

Sunny Hill Festival, the Dua Lipa-co-founded festival in Prishtina, Kosovo (July 31–Aug. 2), has named Fever its official international ticketing partner for 2026 to broaden access for overseas fans. The 2025 edition drew 100,000+ attendees from 60+ countries.

### Key Points
- Fever named official international ticketing partner for the 2026 edition.
- 2025 festival drew over 100,000 attendees from 60+ countries, with €22M in direct economic impact cited.
- 2026 lineup includes Katy Perry, Martin Garrix, and Lewis Capaldi.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Sunny-Hill-Festival.webp)

Sunny Hill Festival has named Fever its official international ticketing partner for the 2026 edition of the event, which will take place in Prishtina, Kosovo, from July 31 through August 2.

Festival organizers said the partnership is intended to make it easier for international fans to purchase tickets and attend the three-day event while supporting cultural exchange and regional tourism.

Sunny Hill Festival welcomed more than 100,000 attendees from over 60 countries in 2025. Organizers said last year’s event generated 25.1 million (€22 million) in direct economic impact.

“Sunny Hill was always about more than music,” said Dukagjin Dugi Lipa, who co-founded the festival with his daughter, Dua Lipa.

“For a few days every summer, we bring the world to Kosovo — and the whole country feels it,” Lipa said. “The attention the festival brings is exposure no tourism campaign could ever buy, and the value it creates stays at home: in our visitor economy, in our cultural sector, and in the opportunities we open for young people.”

He added that Fever’s international reach and technology platform will help fans around the world discover the festival, purchase tickets and travel to Prishtina.

Founded in 2018, Sunny Hill Festival is promoted internationally by three-time Grammy Award winner Dua Lipa. The festival has grown into one of Kosovo’s largest music events, attracting attendees from around the world.

“Sunny Hill is that rare event where a great festival is also a growth engine for an entire country,” said Mariano Otero, senior vice president at Fever.
