# Issue 142

## Summary

This is IQ Magazine's Issue 142 landing page — a table of contents for the February 2026 issue. Featured content includes Wolf Alice's rise to arena headliners, a profile of Dutch promoter Leon Ramakers' 55-year career, a Middle East market report, a feature on live-event insurance, and a Harlem Globetrotters centennial retrospective.

### Key Points
- "Formidable Cool": how Wolf Alice built the touring operation behind their arena-headliner run.
- "The Architects: Leon Ramakers" profiles the Mojo co-founder's 55 years building a Dutch touring hub.
- "Arabian Nights" is a Middle East market-focus report on the region's live-entertainment business.
- "Take Cover!" examines the importance of adequate insurance for live-entertainment workers amid industry uncertainty.
- "Slam Dunk!" looks back on the Harlem Globetrotters' centennial history.
- Additional pieces cover Japanese market strategy and the value of touring data.
