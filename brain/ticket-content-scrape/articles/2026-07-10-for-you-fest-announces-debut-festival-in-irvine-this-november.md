# For You Fest Announces Debut Festival in Irvine This November

## Summary

Creator-focused For You Fest will hold its inaugural event Nov. 14-15 at Great Park in Irvine, California, combining live podcasts, comedy, music and interactive experiences across three stages. Lineup includes Quenlin Blackwell, Tana Mongeau, Trisha Paytas, Drew Afualo, Meg Stalter and others.

### Key Points
- Debut festival: Nov 14-15, Great Park, Irvine, CA.
- Three stages: Podcast & Comedy, Music, Beauty & Fashion.
- Headline creators include Tana Mongeau, Trisha Paytas, Drew Afualo, Meg Stalter, Owen Thiele.
- Format blends live podcast recordings with music and beauty/fashion masterclasses.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/For-You-Fest-1.webp)

For You Fest will launch its inaugural event November 14 to 15 at Great Park in Irvine, California, bringing together creators and fans for two days of live podcasts, comedy, music, workshops and interactive experiences.

The festival will feature appearances from Quenlin Blackwell, Tana Mongeau, DJ Trixie Mattel, Trisha Paytas, Patrick Ta, Wisdom Kaye, Caleb Hearon, Drew Afualo, Meg Stalter and Owen Thiele, with additional creators expected to be announced.

Programming will take place across three stages dedicated to Podcast & Comedy, Music, and Beauty & Fashion.

The Podcast & Comedy stage will host live recordings of Quenlin Blackwell’s “Feeding Starving Celebrities,” Tana Mongeau’s “Brand Safe” and a performance from Owen Thiele. Comedy sets from Caleb Hearon, Drew Afualo and Meg Stalter are also scheduled. On the Music stage, DJ Trixie Mattel, Trisha Paytas and Meg Stalter will perform.

Patrick Ta and Wisdom Kaye will lead beauty and fashion masterclasses as part of the Beauty & Fashion stage programming.

In addition to the stage performances, attendees can purchase add-on Culinary and Beauty & Fashion Workshops. These sessions will provide hands-on instruction from chefs, creators and industry professionals in fully equipped workshop spaces.

The festival will also offer Meet & Greets, giving attendees the opportunity to spend one-on-one time with participating creators, take photos and create content together.

Presale tickets for 2-Day and 1-Day General Admission, Pit Viewing, VIP, Besties and Suite packages will be available beginning July 10 at 10 a.m. PT. A general public on-sale will follow if tickets remain available.
