# Tarleton State University Selects Oak View Group For Event Bookings Support At EECU Center

## Summary

Tarleton State University announced a partnership with Oak View Group (OVG) to help secure live event bookings at its new 10,000-seat EECU Center in Stephenville, Texas. The arena hosts NCAA Division I basketball, conferences, concerts, and community events; OVG already holds similar college-venue booking relationships at several other universities.

### Key Points
- EECU Center: 148,000 sq. ft., two-story multipurpose arena; basketball capacity 8,000 (portable Summy Court flooring), concert/event capacity up to 10,000.
- OVG will provide booking support to attract concerts and events to the venue.
- Quotes from David Martin (Tarleton State AVP for Campus Operations) and Peter Luukko (OVG Co-Chair).
- OVG has comparable booking relationships at NC State, University of Tennessee, University of Tulsa, Iowa State, and University of Arkansas.
- OVG also operates venues at Arizona State, University of Houston, University of Texas, UMass Amherst, University of Rhode Island, and Saint Louis University.
- Oak View Group is disclosed as Pollstar's parent company.

Tarleton State University today announced a strategic partnership with Oak View Group to help secure additional live event bookings at the new 10,000-seat EECU Center.

“We’re thankful for the partnership with Oak View Group. They have a strong track record of securing live events, which will bring great visibility to the EECU Center in Stephenville,” said David Martin, Tarleton State University Assistant Vice President for Campus Operations, “They have an experienced team, and we look forward to working with them as we create opportunities on our campus and in the community.”

“Stephenville has established itself as a destination for live music and we’re excited to partner with Tarleton State University to unlock the full potential of EECU Center,” said Peter Luukko, Co-Chair, Oak View Group. “Together, we’re well-positioned to attract concerts and events that will enhance the university experience and create new energy for the broader community.”

Featuring 148,000 square feet of space, the EECU Center is a two-story multipurpose arena that hosts NCAA Division I basketball, conferences, concerts, conventions and community events, serving as a new hub for the university and the region. Basketball games are played on Summy Court, a portable maple wood flooring system that allows for efficient installation and removal while accommodating up to 8,000 fans. Concerts and other uses can welcome up to 10,000 spectators.

OVG has similar booking relationships at NC State University (Raleigh, NC); University of Tennessee (Knoxville, TN); University of Tulsa in Tulsa, OK; Iowa State University in Ames, IA and the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville, AR. OVG also operates a number of top college venues including Arizona State University in Tempe, AZ; University of Houston in Houston, TX; University of Texas in Austin, TX; University of Massachusetts in Amherst, MA; University of Rhode Island in Kingston, RI; and Saint Louis University in St. Louis, MO.
