# Carlos Santana Adds November 2026 Residency Dates

## Summary

Carlos Santana has added new dates to his long-running Las Vegas residency at House of Blues inside Mandalay Bay, now in its 14th year. New shows run Nov. 4-15, following a previously announced September run. Tickets go on sale July 11 at 10am PT.

### Key Points
- New residency dates: Nov 4-5, 7-8, 11-12, 14-15, 2026.
- Residency now in its 14th year at Mandalay Bay's House of Blues.
- Presented by SiriusXM.
- Tickets on sale Saturday, July 11 at 10am PT.

Jackpot! Carlos Santana has added another round of dates to his Las Vegas residency, “An Intimate Evening with Santana: Greatest Hits Live: Presented by SiriusXM at House of Blues inside Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino.”

Santana has been blessing Sin City for over a decade, with the residency now in its 14th year.

Following a previously announced run of September shows, the new dates take place November 4- 5, 7-8, 11-12 and 14-15. Tickets go on sale Saturday, July 11 at 10 a.m. PT

A write-up about the residency from Las Vegas Magazine declares, “In the hands of Carlos Santana, a guitar becomes a conduit to the divine. For 13 years, the 76-year-old virtuoso has transformed the House of Blues at Mandalay Bay into something approaching a temple, where his An Intimate Evening with Santana: Greatest Hits Live residency has turned from mere entertainment into something approaching a musical pilgrimage.”

The review adds, “What makes this residency endure isn’t just the hits, though Santana has an arsenal of them. From “Black Magic Woman” and “Oye Como Va” to “Maria Maria” and “Smooth,” each track arrives timeless and electric. Santana doesn’t perform these songs; he channels them, letting each solo spiral into uncharted musical territory.”

2026 Performances (all shows scheduled to begin at 8 p.m.):  
Sept. 2026: 16, 17, 19, 20, 23, 24, 26, 27 – on sale now  
Nov. 2026: 4, 5, 7, 8, 11, 12, 14, 15 – just added!
