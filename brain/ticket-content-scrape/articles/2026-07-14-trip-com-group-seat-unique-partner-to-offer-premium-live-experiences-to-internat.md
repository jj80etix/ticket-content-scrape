# Trip.com Group, Seat Unique partner to offer premium live experiences to international travellers

## Summary

Hospitality and VIP-package provider Seat Unique has partnered with Trip.com Group to sell official hospitality/VIP packages for Formula 1, the Premier League, major music tours, and other live events across Trip.com's international travel platforms. The deal marks Seat Unique's first major Asia-Pacific expansion, citing Trip.com research that two-thirds of Asia-Pacific travellers have planned trips around concerts.

### Key Points
- Seat Unique's official hospitality/VIP packages become a new category on Trip.com Group's platforms.
- Deal is Seat Unique's first major push into the Asia-Pacific region.
- Cited research: 66% of Asia-Pacific travellers surveyed are willing to travel internationally for a favorite artist; two-thirds have planned holidays around concerts.
- Covers premium access to European sporting, music, and entertainment events for international travellers.

TheTicketingBusiness News

**Seat Unique has agreed a deal to bring its official hospitality and VIP packages to travellers worldwide.**

Partnering with global travel service provider Trip.com Group, premium live events will be introduced as a new category across Trip.com Group’s platforms. Customers will have access to official hospitality and VIP packages across Formula 1, the Premier League, major music tours and other live events.

The deal also forms part of Seat Unique’s international growth strategy, and marks the group’s first major expansion into the Asia-Pacific region.

**Event-led travel**

Research from Trip.com Group found that two thirds of Asia-Pacific travellers planned their holidays around concerts at least once, with 66% willing to travel internationally to watch their favourite artists live.

Football, basketball and Formula 1 are also some of the most popular live sports to watch while travelling.

The partnership will provide access for international travellers to premium experiences at a range of Europe’s sporting, music and entertainment events through Trip.com Group’s all-in-one platform.

“The way people travel is changing. Increasingly, it’s no longer just about where you go, but what you experience when you get there,” said Robin Sherry, chief executive of Seat Unique Group.

“By partnering with Trip.com Group, we’re giving millions of travellers access to official premium experiences at some of the world’s biggest sport, music and entertainment events through a platform they already know and trust. This partnership marks a significant milestone for our business.

“It accelerates our international growth, helps our rights-holder partners reach millions of new premium customers and brings unforgettable live experiences within reach of a truly global audience.”

Bo Sun, chief marketing officer of Trip.com Group added: “Experience-led travel is reshaping how people plan their journeys, and travellers are increasingly building trips around the moments that matter most to them.

“Live events are a powerful expression of that shift, and through this partnership we’re bringing fans closer to some of the world’s most exciting sports, music, and entertainment experiences.

“This marks another step in our journey to unlock new possibilities between travel and experiences, bringing greater value to travellers and destinations worldwide.”

[Last month](https://www.theticketingbusiness.com/2026/06/seat-unique-raises-20m-to-accelerate-growth-international-expansion/), it was announced that Seat Unique had raised £20m (€23m/$26.5m) in a new funding round, led by Nickleby Capital.

The finance will support further acquisitions, international expansion and continued growth across premium ticketing, hospitality and travel.
