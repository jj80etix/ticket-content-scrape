# Alice Cooper Sets 2026 North American Headlining Tour

## Summary

Alice Cooper announced a North American headlining tour running October 27 (Clearwater, FL) through November 21 (Reno, NV), featuring his "Alice's Attic" stage production, plus September festival dates including Louder Than Life and CityFolk.

### Key Points
- Headlining run: October 27 (Clearwater) through November 21 (Reno).
- September festival dates: Louder Than Life (KY), The Big E Fair (MA), CityFolk Festival (Ottawa).
- Tour supports his 2025 album *The Revenge of Alice Cooper*, reuniting original band members.
- Features the "Alice's Attic" production debuted on his 2025 Judas Priest co-headline tour.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Alice-Cooper-Alices-Attic.webp)

Alice Cooper is getting ready to hit the road, bringing his “Alice’s Attic” stage production to cities across the United States and Canada.

The headlining run is set to begin on October 27 in Clearwater, Florida, at Ruth Eckerd Hall. From there, the 78 years old rocker is scheduled to make stops in cities such as Richmond, Atlantic City, Hershey, St. Louis, San Diego and San Jose before wrapping up on November 21 in Reno, Nevada at the Grand Theatre at Grand Sierra Resort + Casino.

The concerts will feature Cooper’s “Alice’s Attic” stage production, which debuted during his co-headlining tour with Judas Priest last year.

Notably, before launching the headlining tour, Cooper will make several festival appearances in September. He is slated to perform at Louder Than Life on September 17 in Kentucky as well as The Big E Fair on September 19 in West Springfield. Cooper is also set to perform at CityFolk Festival in Ottawa on September 20.

The tour follows the release of Cooper’s latest album, *The Revenge of Alice Cooper*, which arrived in 2025. The album reunited Cooper with members of the original Alice Cooper Band for the first time in decades.

For more information and additional details, fans can visit [Cooper’s official website](https://alicecooper.com/).
