# Latto to Join Doja Cat on North American Leg of ‘Tour Ma Vie World Tour’

## Summary

Doja Cat has added Atlanta rapper Latto as a special guest on all 31 North American dates of her "Tour Ma Vie World Tour," in support of Doja Cat's album *Vie*. The North American leg runs October 1 (Little Caesars Arena, Detroit) through December 1 (Madison Square Garden, New York), with stops including Chicago, Denver, Vancouver, Seattle, Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Houston, Miami and Boston. The pairing follows Latto and Doja Cat's recent collaboration "Okayyy," from Latto's album *Big Mama*; tickets are on sale now.

### Key Points
- Latto joins all 31 North American dates of Doja Cat's "Tour Ma Vie World Tour."
- Tour runs October 1, 2026 (Detroit) to December 1, 2026 (New York, Madison Square Garden), across major arenas in the US and Canada.
- Announcement ties to Latto/Doja Cat's collaboration "Okayyy" from Latto's album *Big Mama*.
- Doja Cat's tour supports her album *Vie*.
- Tickets are already on sale via Latto's official website.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Doja-Cat-with-Latto.webp)

Doja Cat has announced that Latto will join the North American leg of the “Tour Ma Vie World Tour” as a special guest.

The Atlanta rapper will perform on all 31 North American dates of the tour, which begins October 1 at Little Caesars Arena in Detroit. From there, the run is scheduled to make stops in cities such as Chicago, Kansas City, Denver, Vancouver, Seattle, Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Houston, Miami and Boston before wrapping up on December 1 in New York at Madison Square Garden.

The tour announcement comes as Doja Cat prepares to hit the road in support of her latest album, *Vie*.

Latto joins the lineup following the recent release of the music video for “Okayyy,” her collaboration with Doja Cat. The song appears on Latto’s latest album, *Big Mama*, and marks the pair’s newest musical collaboration ahead of their upcoming tour together.
