# Cheap Trick Adds Fall 2026 Dates to ‘All Washed Up’ Tour

## Summary

Cheap Trick added a second leg of "All Washed Up" tour dates beginning Nov. 5 in Fort Wayne, Indiana, extending the previously announced summer/fall trek through a Nov. 22 close in Detroit.

### Key Points
- New leg: Nov. 5–22, ten additional Midwest/Northeast dates.
- Original leg runs Aug. 2–Oct. 4 across the U.S.
- Stops include Milwaukee, St. Louis, and Cincinnati.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/image_2026-07-21_164105463.png)

Cheap Trick fans have another chance to see the Rock and Roll Hall of Famers this fall, as the group just revealed a handful of addition dates on their “All Washed Up” tour.

The previously-announced trek kicks-off August 2 at Rockin’ the Fields of Minnedosa, followed by gigs across the U.S. in cities like Modesto, Los Angeles, Aurora, Lincoln, Louisville, Nashville, and The Woodlands, running through early October.

Now, the band added another leg, set to begin on November 5 in Fort Wayne, Indiana. It’ll make stops in Madison, St. Louis, La Vista, and Cincinnati, stopping at venues along the way like Davenport’s Capitol Theatre, the Landmark Credit Union Live in Milwaukee, and MGM Northfield Park Center Stage in Northfield before wrapping-up at Detroit’s Sound Board Theater on November 22.

Formed in Rockford, Illinois in the 1970s, Cheap Trick have built a lasting legacy with classic rock staples like “I Want You to Want Me” and “Surrender.”
