# Eli Young Band Sets North American ‘Keep On Dreaming’ Tour

## Summary

Country group Eli Young Band announced its headlining "Keep On Dreaming Tour" across the US and Canada this fall, running August 6 through October 23. Tickets for the newly announced dates go on sale July 10.

### Key Points
- Tour runs Aug 6 (Washington, D.C.) to Oct 23 (Aurora, IL).
- Stops include Reading PA, Vancouver BC, Des Moines IA, Crystal Beach TX, and Baton Rouge LA.
- Tickets go on sale July 10 at 10am local time.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Eli-Young-Band-Keep-on-Dreaming-tour.webp)

Eli Young Band has announced its headlining Keep On Dreaming Tour, which will take the country group across the United States and Canada this fall.

The tour begins on August 6 at The Bullpen in Washington, D.C. From there, the band is scheduled to make stops in cities such as Reading, PA, Vancouver, BC, Des Moines, IA, Crystal Beach, TX and Baton Rouge, LA before wrapping up on October 23 in Aurora, IL, at Hollywood Casino.

Taking to social media, the band shared:

“Almost fifteen years ago, we released Life At Best. We never could’ve imagined songs like “Crazy Girl” and “Even If It Breaks Your Heart” would become part of so many of your stories.
