# SUNN O))) Announces Fall Tour Dates

## Summary

SUNN O))) has announced a 25-date fall 2026 North American tour supporting its self-titled Sub Pop debut album, running October 2 through November 1. The trek follows a successful spring/summer run and hits theaters and clubs across the U.S. and Canada. Tickets for the new dates go on sale July 17, with the band represented by UTA in North America.

### Key Points
- 25-show run begins Oct. 2 at Mercury Ballroom in Louisville, KY, and closes Nov. 1 at The Pearl in Vancouver, BC.
- Tour supports SUNN O)))'s self-titled Sub Pop debut album.
- Tickets go on sale July 17, 2026.
- Stops include Cleveland (Globe Iron), Boston (Paradise Rock Club), St. Louis (Delmar Hall), Salt Lake City (First Baptist Church), and Olympia, WA (Capitol Theater), among others.
- SUNN O))) is booked by UTA in North America.

Coming off a successful spring and summer run, drone metal band SUNN O))) has announced fall North American dates in support of its self-titled Sub Pop debut album.

The 25-show theater run begins Oct. 2 at Louisville’s Mercury Ballroom and includes stops in Cleveland, Boston, St. Louis and more before wrapping Nov. 1 at The Pearl in Vancouver.

Tickets for the new dates are on sale July 17. See https://sunn.southernlord.com/tour-data/ for more info.

SUNN O))) is repped by UTA in North America.
