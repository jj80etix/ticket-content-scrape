# Madison Beer Fans Respond to Cheap Ticket Prices

## Summary

Resale tickets for several stops on Madison Beer's "Locket" Tour listed for under $15, sparking social-media commentary; rather than reading it as a bad sign, many fans framed the low prices positively as increased accessibility. Despite the cheap resale listings, the tour itself is performing strongly — 22 of 33 dates sold out and the North American leg ~99% sold out, including an expected sellout finale at Madison Square Garden.

### Key Points
- Resale GA tickets spotted as low as $12.87-$14.04 at some tour stops.
- Social reaction was largely positive, framing cheap resale as improved accessibility rather than a demand failure.
- 22 of 33 tour dates sold out; North American leg ~99% sold out.
- Remaining shows include Toronto (July 10), Boston (July 12), and a Madison Square Garden finale (July 13) expected to sell out.

### Highlights
- Sub-$15 resale prices coexisting with a ~99%-sold-out North American leg shows resale pricing can diverge sharply from demand strength at specific shows/markets, not just track overall sellout status.
- Patron sentiment example: fans publicly framed cheap resale as a positive ("priced out" fans getting access), a useful counter-narrative to junk-fee/price-gouging complaints.

### Industry Problem
Illustrates the volatility and opacity of secondary-market pricing for patrons — resale prices can swing far below face value even for a well-selling tour, which is confusing for fans trying to gauge fair value and for artists/promoters trying to protect perceived exclusivity.

### Proposed Solution
Reinforces the case for real-time, transparent resale pricing/comparison tools so patrons (and Etix's own clients) can distinguish "cheap because oversupplied at this stop" from "cheap because scalped inventory is dumping" — useful grounding context for patron-facing pricing communication.

Madison Beer is drawing attention online after resale tickets for her “Locket” Tour were listed for less than $15 at some shows.

The 27-year-old singer has been touring since May in support of her latest album, with the 33-date run visiting cities across Europe, the United Kingdom and North America.

Earlier this month, an X user shared a screenshot showing general admission resale tickets priced at $12.87 and $14.04, writing, “Should I see Madison Beer or go to Taco Bell?”

While another user shared, “going to see madison beer on thursday because the tickets were less than a starbucks drink”
