# Garth Brooks Expands ‘Blame It All On My Roots’ Tour with Denver Stop

## Summary

Garth Brooks has added two Denver-area dates to his "Blame It All On My Roots" arena tour, playing Ball Arena on September 4 and 5. The announcement follows an earlier expansion of the tour's Indianapolis opening leg to four consecutive shows after strong demand.

### Key Points
- Brooks will play Ball Arena in Denver on Friday, September 4, and Saturday, September 5, 2026.
- The tour will feature Brooks' signature in-the-round Drum Pod stage setup.
- The tour now opens with four consecutive shows at Gainbridge Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, August 20-23, after additional dates were added there due to demand.
- Tickets for the Denver shows go on sale Friday, July 31, at 10 a.m. MT.
- More information is available at garthbrooks.com.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Garth-Brooks-blame-it-all-on-my-roots-1-1.webp)

Garth Brooks is expanding his upcoming “Blame It All On My Roots” arena tour with two newly announced performances at Denver’s Ball Arena. The country music superstar will bring the tour to Colorado for a pair of concerts scheduled for early September.

Brooks will perform at Ball Arena on Friday, September 4, and Saturday, September 5. The Denver shows will be part of the singer’s return to large-scale arena performances, featuring the in-the-round Drum Pod stage that has been closely associated with his live shows throughout his career.

The Denver announcement follows the addition of more shows to the opening leg of Brooks’ tour in Indianapolis, Indiana. The singer recently expanded his launch dates at Gainbridge Fieldhouse after strong fan demand led to additional performances being added.
