# Satisfi Labs launches AI ticketing agent for tourism

## Summary

Satisfi Labs and ticketing platform Ventrata have launched an agentic AI ticketing assistant for the tourism/attractions sector that handles the full purchase journey — discovery, recommendations, checkout, and post-purchase support — inside a single chat conversation. Pilot deployments at City Sightseeing New Orleans and One World Observatory (NYC), live since early 2026, report meaningful conversion and revenue gains, positioning the tool as a concrete benchmark for AI-driven ticketing commerce.

### Key Points
- Satisfi Labs (AI agent) partnered with Ventrata (ticketing infrastructure) to launch a conversational AI agent spanning discovery through post-purchase support.
- Deployed since the start of 2026 as a pilot at City Sightseeing New Orleans and One World Observatory in New York.
- City Sightseeing New Orleans: 1,800+ ticket interactions via the agent, averaging 2.5 tickets per transaction, with 90% of discovery/purchases happening on mobile.
- One World Observatory: weekly in-chat revenue grew 2.5x over eight weeks, averaging 2.4 tickets per transaction with a 3.1% click-to-confirmed-sale rate.
- Client testimonial credits the agent with reducing customer-service staff workload while driving incremental revenue.

### Highlights
- Conversion metrics: 2.5x weekly in-chat revenue growth over 8 weeks at One World Observatory; 3.1% click-to-confirmed-sale rate; 90% mobile share at City Sightseeing New Orleans.
- Agent spans the full funnel — discovery/recommendation, checkout, and post-purchase support — in one chat thread, not a single-purpose bot.
- Built as a layer over Ventrata's existing ticketing commerce infrastructure via partnership/integration, not a ground-up rebuild.
- Live pilot since start of 2026 at two named attraction operators, giving real (if early) usage data rather than a demo.

### Industry Problem
Attraction and tourism operators face both weak online conversion (browsers who don't purchase) and high customer-support overhead; this deployment evidences demand for AI that collapses discovery-to-purchase friction while simultaneously deflecting support volume from staff.

### Proposed Solution
Satisfi/Ventrata addressed this by layering an agentic, chat-based AI directly over ticketing commerce for discovery, checkout, and support rather than shipping a narrow FAQ bot. For Etix Assistant, this is a direct benchmark: prioritize a full-funnel conversational agent and track the same metrics (tickets/transaction, mobile conversion, click-to-confirmed-sale rate, support deflection) this deployment reports.

**Satisfi Labs has launched an AI ticketing agent for tourism in partnership with ticketing solutions company Ventrata.**

The tool has been designed to handle the full ticket journey inside a single chat conversation, from discovery and recommendations to checkout and post-purchase support.
