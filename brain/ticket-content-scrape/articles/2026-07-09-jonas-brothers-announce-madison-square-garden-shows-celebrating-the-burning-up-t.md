# Jonas Brothers Announce Madison Square Garden Shows Celebrating the ‘Burning Up Tour’

## Summary

The Jonas Brothers announced a three-night "Burning Up All Over" run at Madison Square Garden in August, celebrating the 20th anniversary of their 2008 "Burning Up Tour," with a third date added due to demand. The shows coincide with the upcoming premiere of Camp Rock 3 on Disney.

### Key Points
- Three MSG shows August 20-22, 2026, celebrating the 2008 "Burning Up Tour."
- Third date added after strong initial demand.
- Timed to the release of Camp Rock 3.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Jonas-Brothers-The-Burning-Up-Tour.webp)

The Jonas Brothers have announced a three-night run at Madison Square Garden this August to celebrate their 2008 “Burning Up Tour.”

Kevin, Joe and Nick Jonas will perform the “Burning Up All Over” shows on August 20, 21 and 22 at the New York venue. The concerts were originally scheduled for August 20 and 21 before a third date was added due to demand.

The performances are being promoted as “a one-of-a-kind event” celebrating the band’s 2008 arena tour while marking 20 years since the Jonas Brothers formed.

“The original ‘Burning Up Tour’ was an exhilarating time in our lives, and getting the chance to relive that with the fans — while celebrating everything that’s happened since — means the world to us,” the band said in a statement.

The original “Burning Up Tour” supported the band’s third studio album, *A Little Bit Longer*, and followed the release of the Disney Channel Original Movie *Camp Rock*. The tour became one of the group’s biggest headlining outings during the early years of its career.

The Madison Square Garden concerts come as *Camp Rock 3* is scheduled to premiere on Disney in August. The shows also coincide with the Jonas Brothers being inducted as Disney Legends.

Several presales will take place before tickets become available to the general public. A Chase cardmember presale begins Wednesday, July 8, at 10 a.m. ET. Additional presales will be held throughout the week before the general onsale.

Tickets go on sale to the general public beginning Friday, July 10, at 10 a.m. ET through Ticketmaster.
