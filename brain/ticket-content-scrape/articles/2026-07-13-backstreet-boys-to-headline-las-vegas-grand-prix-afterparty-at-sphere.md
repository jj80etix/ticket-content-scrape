# Backstreet Boys to headline Las Vegas Grand Prix afterparty at Sphere

## Summary

The Backstreet Boys will headline the official Formula 1 Las Vegas Grand Prix afterparty at Sphere on November 21, sold as a package with race-weekend general admission starting at $925.

### Key Points
- Backstreet Boys headline the F1 Las Vegas Grand Prix afterparty at Sphere, November 21.
- Ticket packages start at $925 and bundle three-day race general admission.
- Fourth edition of the Las Vegas Grand Prix, taking place November 19-21.

![TheTicketingBusiness News](https://www.theticketingbusiness.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/TBN-LOGO-reversed-white.png)

TheTicketingBusiness News

**The Backstreet Boys are set to perform at the official post-race show of the Formula 1 Las Vegas Grand Prix at Sphere on November 21.**

Fans will be able to attend the afterparty with a ticket package that also includes general admission to the three-day motorsport event, with prices starting at $925 (£690/€810).

“Las Vegas is the only place in the world where Formula 1, the Backstreet Boys and Sphere can come together for one unforgettable night,” read a Formula 1 statement.

“F1 Afterparty reflects what makes the Las Vegas Grand Prix so distinctive, giving fans the opportunity to see an iconic group like the Backstreet Boys perform at Sphere as part of a race weekend experience you can’t find anywhere else.

“As Las Vegas welcomes visitors from around the world for Formula 1 weekend, Sphere will once again deliver entertainment at a scale unmatched by any other venue.

“F1 Afterparty will feature the Backstreet Boys, whose critically acclaimed, sold-out Sphere performances exemplify our commitment to bringing iconic artists together with groundbreaking technology to create experiences only Sphere can deliver.”

The total race weekend is scheduled to take place from November 19 to 21 and will be the fourth edition of the grand prix.
