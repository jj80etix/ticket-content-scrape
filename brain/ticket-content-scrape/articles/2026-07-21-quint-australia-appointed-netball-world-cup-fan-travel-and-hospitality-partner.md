# Quint Australia appointed Netball World Cup fan travel and hospitality partner

## Summary

Netball World Cup Sydney 2027 has named Quint Australia its exclusive fan travel and hospitality partner, adding the August 2027 tournament to Quint's portfolio alongside the NBA, WNBA, F1, and Australian Open.

### Key Points
- Partnership covers premium hospitality and travel packages for the Aug. 25–Sept. 5, 2027 tournament at Afterpay Arena.
- Fan access will run through a network of official travel agents.
- Quint's existing portfolio includes NBA, WNBA, F1, and the Australian Open.

## Transcript

![TheTicketingBusiness News](https://www.theticketingbusiness.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/TBN-LOGO-reversed-white.png)

TheTicketingBusiness News

**Netball World Cup Sydney 2027 (NWC2027) has appointed Quint Australia as its exclusive official fan travel and hospitality partner.**

The collaboration will see Quint design and deliver premium packages, hospitality offerings, and bespoke event experiences for the tournament set to take place from August 25 to September 5 at the Afterpay Arena.

“World Netball, the Netball World Cup Sydney 2027 and Quint Australia share a vision to redefine the fan experience and set a new benchmark for hospitality at netball’s biggest global event,” said NWC2027 chief executive, Mark Falvo.

“Together, we are creating premium, tailored experiences that will resonate with fans across Australia and those travelling from around the world to be part of the Netball World Cup in Sydney.”

The 17 <sup>th</sup> edition of the Netball World Cup joins Quint’s growing partner portfolio, which also includes NBA and WNBA, Formula 1, and the Australian Open.

The partnership will also give fans access to Netball World Cup Sydney 2027 Experiences via a network of appointed official fan travel agents.

“Netball has a passionate, loyal, and growing global fan base, and this partnership gives us the opportunity to deliver something truly special for netball fans,” said Quint Australia CEO Daniel Cecconi.

“Whether you are travelling from within Sydney, interstate or from the other side of the world, Quint Australia want every fan and attendee to have an unforgettable experience from the moment they start planning their Netball World Cup experience to the moment they leave the arena, and beyond!”
