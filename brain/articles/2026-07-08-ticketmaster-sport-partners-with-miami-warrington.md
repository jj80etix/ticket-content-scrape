# Ticketmaster Sport partners with Miami, Warrington

## Summary

Ticketmaster Sport has become the official ticketing partner of University of Miami Athletics (football, basketball, baseball) and Super League rugby side Warrington Wolves, adding two new client wins in the sports vertical on the same day. The Miami deal bundles ticket sales, resale, transfer and entry via Ticketmaster.com, the university's site, and its app, plus a "community partner" program giving underserved South Florida youth access to games. Warrington's own statement frames the switch to Ticketmaster as driven by faster checkout and a "modern platform," implying dissatisfaction with its prior ticketing setup.

### Key Points
- Ticketmaster Sport is now the official ticketing partner for University of Miami Athletics (football, basketball, baseball).
- Fans buy/sell/transfer/access Miami tickets via Ticketmaster.com, MiamiHurricanes.com, or the Miami Hurricanes app.
- Ticketmaster also becomes a "community partner," providing underserved South Florida youth access to Miami athletics events.
- Warrington Wolves (UK Super League rugby) moves its ticketing to Ticketmaster, citing faster checkout and simpler ticket access.
- Warrington's statement frames the move as "an important step in improving the overall matchday experience," implying a switch away from a prior provider.
- The deals expand Ticketmaster's sports-ticketing footprint into US college athletics and UK rugby league on the same day.

### Highlights
- Two new Ticketmaster Sport client wins announced together: University of Miami Athletics (US college sports) and Warrington Wolves (UK Super League rugby).
- Warrington's public statement explicitly cites faster checkout, easier ticket access, and a "modern platform" as the reasons for switching — direct, named client-selection criteria.
- The Miami contract pairs commercial ticketing with a philanthropic access program for underserved youth, layering a CSR angle onto the deal.

### Industry Problem
Warrington's own language ("moving to Ticketmaster... important step in improving... matchday experience") signals its prior ticketing setup fell short on checkout speed and access convenience — the same checkout-friction and platform-modernity factors that drive venue/organizer churn decisions (Q20) and that consumer-major platforms like Ticketmaster are using to out-compete for sports-ticketing contracts (Q12).

### Proposed Solution
Ticketmaster's pitch bundles a modernized checkout/entry stack with community-partnership framing to win the contract. Etix should track which checkout- and entry-speed features, and which community/CSR framing, are cited as deciding factors in venue/team ticketing switches like this, and benchmark Etix's own checkout and entry flows against what's winning these accounts.

TheTicketingBusiness News

**Ticketmaster Sport has become the official ticketing partner of both University of Miami Athletics and rugby league side Warrington Wolves.**

The Miami deal sees the company operate ticketing for the Hurricane’s American football, basketball and baseball programmes.

Hurricanes fans will be able to buy, sell, transfer and access tickets through Ticketmaster.com, MiamiHurricanes.com or through the Miami Hurricanes app.

“We’re focused on creating a seamless ticketing experience at every stage, from purchase through entry, while helping the University strengthen its engagement with fans and the community,” said Ticketmaster’s executive vice president Clay Luter.

“We’re excited to begin this partnership and look forward to the season ahead.”

Ticketmaster will also be a community partner of the Miami Hurricanes providing access to University of Miami athletic programmes to underserved youth throughout South Florida.

**Warrington Wolves**

Super League team Warrington Wolves has also enlisted Ticketmaster in the hope that it can provide a faster and more convenient way for fans to secure tickets.

“Moving to Ticketmaster Sport is an important step in improving the overall matchday experience for our supporters,” read a club statement.

“The new system offers a secure and reliable ticket-buying journey, with faster checkout, easy access to your tickets, and a modern platform that makes planning your visit to The Halliwell Jones Stadium even simpler.”
