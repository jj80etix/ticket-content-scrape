# Live Nation Introduces ‘Local Noise Philadelphia’ Battle of the Bands Competition; Winner To Play Brooklyn Bowl, Freedom Mortgage Pavilion

## Summary

Live Nation launched "Local Noise Philadelphia," a battle-of-the-bands-style competition for local artists, with winners earning a slot at Brooklyn Bowl Philadelphia, an opening slot at Freedom Mortgage Pavilion, and a cash prize; submissions close July 24 ahead of August semifinals and a championship final.

### Key Points
- Submissions open through July 24 for the competition at 900-cap Brooklyn Bowl Philadelphia.
- Semifinals Aug. 13-14, championship final Aug. 20.
- Winner gets a Freedom Mortgage Pavilion tour opening slot plus cash prize.

Local Noise Philadelphia

Live Nation has announced a new Local Noise Philadelphia battle-of-the-bands-style competition to celebrate the next generation of local talent, with winners getting the opportunity to perform at Brooklyn Bowl Philadelphia this August as well as an opening slot at a tour date at Freedom Mortgage Pavilion along with a cash prize.

Bands and artists have until July 24 to submit for the chance to compete live at the 900-capacity Brooklyn Bowl Philadelphia this August.

“Every artist starts somewhere,” the announcement reads. “Local Noise Philadelphia is designed to help the region’s next generation of bands and artists take the next step.”

Select artists will compete in live semifinal performances on Aug. 13-14 at Brooklyn Bowl Philadelphia. The competition will culminate with the championship finals back at the venue on Aug. 20.

Prizes for the winner of the whole shebang include: the opportunity to perform at the 25,000-capacity Freedom Mortgage Pavilion in nearby Camden during a major concert event later this year, a $5,000 cash prize and exposure to new fans and opportunities to build valuable industry connections. Upcoming shows at the venue include everyone from Dave Matthews Band to Melanie Martinez, TLC and Gorillaz.

All bands interested in participating must submit an application through the online portal for a chance to perform. [Official contest rules and eligibility requirements are available here.](https://m.cmpgn.page/hqQVkC)
