# Ken Carson Announces Fall 2026 ‘Xperimenting Tour’

## Summary

Rapper Ken Carson announced a 17-date fall 2026 "Xperimenting Tour" across the US, promoted by Live Nation, kicking off August 26 in Chicago and wrapping September 23 in San Francisco, in support of his new album *xperiment*. He'll also appear at ComplexCon on October 3.

### Key Points
- 17-date US tour runs Aug 26 (Chicago) to Sept 23 (San Francisco), promoted by Live Nation.
- Supports his fifth studio album *xperiment*, featuring Destroy Lonely, Lil Uzi Vert, Young Thug and Playboi Carti.
- Artist presale begins July 8; general on-sale July 10.
- Follows his prior "WTF Tour," which included a sold-out OVO Arena Wembley show grossing $511,164.

Ken Carson is hitting the road this fall with his “xperimenting tour,” promoted by Live Nation and supported by prettifun, DJ Moon, and xaviersobased. The 17-date US trek kicks off on Aug. 26 at Chicago’s Huntington Bank Pavilion. Carson and co. will then make stops in Brooklyn, Washington, D.C., Miami, Atlanta, and more before wrapping on Sept. 23 at Bill Graham Civic Auditorium in San Francisco. Fans will also be able to catch the rising star at ComplexCon on Oct. 3. See full tour routing below.

Artist presale begins Wednesday, July 8, at 10:00 a.m. local time. General on sale starts Friday, July 10, at 10:00 a.m. local time. Tickets are available [here](https://kencarson.xyz/tour/).

Ken Carson previously toured through the EU and the UK as a part of his “WTF Tour” in support of his 2025 project, *More Chaos*. The tour produced a couple of sold-out shows, including a stop at OVO Arena Wembley in London, which grossed $511,164, according to [Pollstar Boxoffice](https://pollstar.com/tour-histories/artists/774698).

The new tour announcement arrives after the Atlanta-based artist delivered his latest album, *xperiment*. Carson’s fifth studio album featured collaborations from Destroy Lonely, Lil Uzi Vert, Young Thug, Playboi Carti, and more.

Additionally, Ken Carson is readying a new mixtape, *cartunez,* which is due out Friday, July 11.
