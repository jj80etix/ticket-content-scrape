# Eagles Extend Sphere Residency, Adding 4 December Shows Due To Overwhelming Demand

## Summary

The Eagles are extending their record-breaking Sphere residency in Las Vegas with four additional December dates due to overwhelming demand, bringing the total run to 68 shows. The residency, Sphere's longest-running, has grossed over $285.5 million from 948,211 tickets across 58 shows so far, with new December tickets starting at $175 on an all-in pricing basis.

### Key Points
- Eagles add four more Sphere shows (Dec 4, 5, 11, 12), bringing the residency total to 68 dates.
- Residency has grossed $285.5M+ off 948,211 tickets across 58 shows since starting September 2024.
- It is Sphere's longest-running residency and recently won Residency of the Year honors.
- New December tickets start at $175 and reflect all-in pricing.
- Artist presale (via Seated) begins July 15; general presales follow July 16.

Las Vegas can’t get enough of the Eagles. The iconic American rock band is extending its record-breaking Sphere residency by adding four December dates due to overwhelming demand.

The four shows take place Dec. 4, 5, 11 and 12, upping the grand total of “Eagles – Live In Concert At Sphere” shows to 68. The band’s remarkable sold-out run at the game-changing venue, which began in September 2024, is Sphere’s longest-running residency, grossing a whopping $285,521,051 off 948,211 tickets across 58 concerts. Artist presale tickets for the December shows go on sale July 15 at 10 a.m. PT. Fans can register for the presale, powered by Seated, at [eagles.com](https://eagles.com/pages/home), and additional presales will begin on July 16 at 10 a.m. PT.

Tickets start at $175 and reflect all-in pricing. Limited VIP packages that include premium seating, exclusive merchandise, parking and more will also be available at [eagles.com](https://eagles.com/pages/home).
