# Théâtre du Lido appoints Secutix as strategic ticketing partner

## Summary

Paris's Théâtre du Lido, a 120,000-visitor-per-year venue on the Champs-Élysées owned by Accor, has appointed Secutix as its strategic ticketing partner, replacing legacy infrastructure with Secutix's cloud-native S-360 platform as part of a roadmap to build a scalable, connected operational ecosystem.

### Highlights
- Théâtre du Lido (120,000+ annual visitors, Accor-owned) switches to Secutix's S-360 cloud-native ticketing platform.
- Deal frames a broader roadmap toward a fully connected ticketing/F&B/operations ecosystem.

### Industry Problem
Legacy on-premise ticketing infrastructure limits venues' ability to scale operations and deepen audience/consumption-habit insight — a modernization gap competitors are actively selling against.

### Proposed Solution
Secutix wins by replacing legacy systems with a unified cloud platform; Etix should track which competitors are winning legacy-replacement deals like this as a signal of where modernization demand is concentrated.

### Key Points
- Secutix named strategic ticketing partner of Théâtre du Lido, Paris, replacing legacy ticketing infrastructure.
- Full-suite rollout covers ticketing (S-360) plus F&B and broader operations.

TheTicketingBusiness News

**Théâtre du Lido has appointed Secutix as its strategic ticketing partner, replacing its legacy ticketing infrastructure with a fully integrated, cloud-native platform.**

Located on the Champs-Élysées in Paris, the venue welcomes more than 120,000 visitors every year.

The pair have collaborated on a clear roadmap outlining their vision of a scalable, fully connected operational ecosystem that will support the venue’s growth ambitions. The aim is to become a major destination for English-language musicals in Paris, building on the progress made following the venue’s acquisition by Accor in 2022.

Under the presidency and artistic direction of Jean-Luc Choplin, the Théâtre du Lido has attracted a significantly renewed audience through productions of *Cabaret*, *Hello, Dolly!*, *The Rocky Horror Show*, *Titanique*  and, since October, *Les Demoiselles de Rochefort*.

In September, Théâtre du Lido will also host a new production of *Singin’ in the Rain*.

The structured approach will help to streamline day-to-day management and operational tools, while deepening knowledge of its audience and their consumption habits.

Théâtre du Lido will benefit from the full suite of Secutix services, covering ticketing via the S-360 solution, F&B/Retail add-on module, integrated access control, and Secutix Audience Management, its marketing module.

The ecosystem will be further enhanced through progressive integrations with CRM, Business Intelligence, and Revenue Management tools, loyalty partners, and interfaces with distributors, resellers, and online travel agencies.

“Our team is proud to support the Théâtre du Lido in its development strategy,” said Adèle Foret, sales manager, live entertainment at Secutix France.

“We are delighted that it is making the most of the best of our technologies and innovations to create unforgettable moments and set a new benchmark in ticketing within the theatre industry.”
