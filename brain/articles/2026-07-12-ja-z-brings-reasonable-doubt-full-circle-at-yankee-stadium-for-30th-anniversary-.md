# JAŸ-Z Brings ‘Reasonable Doubt’ Full Circle At Yankee Stadium For 30th Anniversary Show (Review)

## Summary

JAY-Z opened a sold-out three-night stand at Yankee Stadium on July 10, 2026, celebrating the 30th anniversary of *Reasonable Doubt* — his first time headlining the venue solo. The 45,000-capacity show (promoted by Roc Nation in partnership with Live Nation) featured surprise guests Beyoncé, Nas, Alicia Keys, Blue Ivy, Jaz-O, and Memphis Bleek, plus a full setlist and staging recap.

### Key Points
- Show sold out at 45,000-capacity Yankee Stadium; first solo headline set there for JAY-Z (previously co-headlined with Eminem in 2010, guested in 2009 and 2013).
- Promoted by Roc Nation in partnership with Live Nation.
- Guest performers included Beyoncé, Nas, Alicia Keys, Blue Ivy, Jaz-O, and Memphis Bleek.
- Full setlist and stage production (video intro, live strings, LED display) detailed in the review.
