# Elton John Plots Two Farewell Concerts in Mexico City

## Summary

Elton John will play two "Farewell to Mexico" concerts at Estadio Banorte in Mexico City on October 2 and 3, his first shows there in 14 years and his first full arena dates since concluding his "Farewell Yellow Brick Road" tour in 2023. The Latin American leg of that farewell tour had been canceled during the COVID-19 pandemic, making this a long-delayed return.

### Key Points
- Two shows announced: Estadio Banorte, Mexico City, Oct. 2 and Oct. 3, 2026.
- First Mexico City performances in 14 years; first full arena dates since his 2023 "Farewell Yellow Brick Road" tour ended.
- Banorte Bank cardholder pre-sale runs July 13 (12:00 p.m.) through July 14 (11:59 p.m.) local time.
- Rocket Club (artist fan club) pre-sale opens Wednesday, July 15 at 12:00 p.m. local time.
- General on-sale begins Thursday, July 16 at 12:00 p.m. local time.
- Ticketing details available via eltonjohn.com.

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Elton-John-Farewell-to-Mexico.webp)

Elton John will return to Mexico City this fall for two concerts billed as his “Farewell to Mexico,” marking his first performances in the city in 14 years.

John will perform at Estadio Banorte on October 2 and October 3. The concerts come three years after he concluded his “Farewell Yellow Brick Road” tour, which was promoted as his final global tour.

Since ending that tour in 2023, John has continued to make select live appearances, including private events, benefit concerts and one-off performances. However, he has not announced a full arena concert since retiring from touring.

The Mexico City dates also give John the opportunity to perform for fans in the region after plans to bring his farewell tour to Latin America were canceled during the COVID-19 pandemic.
