# Mariska Hargitay Announces Three California Performances of ‘Every Brilliant Thing’

## Summary

Mariska Hargitay will bring her Broadway production of "Every Brilliant Thing" to Palo Alto's Lucie Stern Theatre for three benefit performances on August 7 and 8, opening TheatreWorks Silicon Valley's 23rd Annual New Works Festival. Proceeds support both TheatreWorks and the Joyful Heart Foundation, the anti-violence nonprofit Hargitay founded. The engagement follows her limited Broadway run at the Hudson Theatre, which concluded July 5 before Tracee Ellis Ross took over the role.

### Key Points
- Three benefit performances of "Every Brilliant Thing" scheduled for August 7-8 at Palo Alto's Lucie Stern Theatre.
- The run opens TheatreWorks Silicon Valley's 23rd Annual New Works Festival.
- Proceeds benefit TheatreWorks Silicon Valley and the Joyful Heart Foundation.
- Follows Hargitay's Broadway debut/limited run at the Hudson Theatre, which ended July 5; Tracee Ellis Ross continues the Broadway run starting July 7.
- Tickets available through TheatreWorks.org.

Mariska Hargitay will bring her Broadway production of “Every Brilliant Thing” to California this summer for a three-performance benefit engagement supporting TheatreWorks Silicon Valley and the Joyful Heart Foundation.

The performances are scheduled for August 7 and 8 at Palo Alto’s Lucie Stern Theatre and will serve as the opening event for TheatreWorks Silicon Valley’s 23rd Annual New Works Festival.

The special engagement will raise funds for both TheatreWorks and the Joyful Heart Foundation, an organization founded by Hargitay that supports survivors of sexual assault, domestic violence and child abuse.
