# Noah Kahan, Jay-Z, Willie Nelson Lead This Week’s Hot Tickets

## Summary

TicketNews/MEGASeats weekly "hot tickets" list is led by Noah Kahan's two Fenway Park dates, followed by Willie Nelson and Family at Pacific Amphitheatre, Jay-Z's Yankee Stadium shows, and the Lakefront Music Fest lineup featuring Keith Urban and Jackson Dean.

### Key Points
- Noah Kahan's July 10-11 Fenway Park shows top the week's demand rankings.
- Willie Nelson and Family, Jay-Z (Yankee Stadium), and Lakefront Music Fest also feature prominently.
