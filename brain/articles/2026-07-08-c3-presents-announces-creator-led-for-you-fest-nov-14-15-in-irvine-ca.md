# C3 Presents Announces Creator-Led For You Fest, Nov. 14-15 In Irvine CA

## Summary

C3 Presents and Live Nation are launching For You Fest, billed as the first large-scale festival built around creator culture, running November 14-15 at the Great Park in Irvine, CA. The event blends live podcast tapings, creator-led workshops, comedy, music, and beauty/fashion programming across three stages, with an inaugural lineup drawn from social-media influencers and podcasters (Tana Mongeau, Trisha Paytas, Drew Afualo, and others) rather than traditional touring artists. Presale begins July 10 with a one-hour guaranteed-lowest-price 2-day GA window before a general onsale.

### Key Points
- New Live Nation/C3 Presents festival, "For You Fest," debuts Nov. 14-15, 2026, at the Great Park in Irvine, CA.
- Positioned as the first large-scale festival organized around creator/influencer culture rather than a traditional music lineup.
- Three stages (Podcast & Comedy, Music, Beauty & Fashion) plus a "Trend Market" retail area and Food & Bev Village.
- Lineup drawn from social-media influencers and podcasters (Quenlin Blackwell, Tana Mongeau, Trisha Paytas, Drew Afualo, Meg Stalter, etc.), not conventional touring musicians.
- Presale opens July 10 at 10 a.m. PT with a one-hour guaranteed-lowest-price 2-day GA window; general onsale follows "if tickets remain."
- Multiple ticket tiers offered, including VIP suites for 15 guests per day.

### Highlights
- A top-tier promoter (Live Nation/C3 Presents) is committing a large-scale, multi-stage festival format entirely to creator/influencer-led programming rather than music headliners — a concrete bet on a new demand-side content category.
- Ticket strategy includes a scarcity-driven, one-hour guaranteed-lowest-price GA window ahead of general onsale, signaling a deliberate demand/pricing experiment for this new format.

### Industry Problem
This tests whether "creator culture" (podcasts, influencers, beauty/comedy content) can anchor a ticketed live event on its own, pointing to a broader demand-side shift where audiences increasingly follow individual creators rather than bands or artists — a potential new inventory category and audience base beyond traditional touring that ticketing platforms may need to serve.

### Proposed Solution
Live Nation/C3 Presents' answer is a purpose-built, multi-stage creator festival with tiered/scarcity pricing. Etix should treat this as a monitor/evolve signal: watch whether For You Fest sells through and whether other promoters replicate the creator-led festival model, since a validated format could open a new client category (podcast networks, creator collectives) worth courting.

Produced by C3 Presents and Live Nation, the new For You Fest looks to turn the social feed into a live festival experience, billing itself as the first large-scale festival built around creator culture and touching Comedy, Music, Beauty, Fashion, Culinary and podcasts.

Taking place at the Great Park in Irvine, California, Nov. 14-15, fans will be able to watch live podcast tapings, attend creator-led workshops, enjoy music and comedy performances and meet the creators they follow online, according to the announcement.

The inaugural festival lineup includes influencers like Quenlin Blackwell, Tana Mongeau, DJ Trixie Mattel, Trisha Paytas, Patrick Ta, Wisdom Kaye, Caleb Hearon, Drew Afualo, Meg Stalter, Owen Thiele and more.

The presale for all ticket types begins Friday, July 10 at 10 a.m. PT with guaranteed lowest price 2-Day GA Tickets available for one hour only. A general onsale will follow “if tickets remain.”

For You Fest will feature programming on three stages (Podcast & Comedy, Music, Beauty & Fashion), including live podcast performances with Quenlin Blackwell (“Feeding Starving Celebrities”), Tana Mongeau (“Brand Safe”), and Owen Thiele, comedy sets from Caleb Hearon & Drew Afualo and Meg Stalter, musical performances by DJ Trixie Mattel, Trisha Paytas and Meg Stalter, and beauty and fashion masterclasses with Patrick Ta and Wisdom Kaye.

In addition to live activities across the stages, fans can enjoy shopping in the Trend Market, where they can discover, sample and shop buzzworthy brands, and enjoy crave-worthy bites and sips at the Food & Bev Village. Multiple ticket packages and VIP options are available, including suites with private space for 15 guests per day.
