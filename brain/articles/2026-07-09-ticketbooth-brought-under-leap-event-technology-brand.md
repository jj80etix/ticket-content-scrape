# Ticketbooth brought under Leap Event Technology brand

## Summary

Australian ticketing business Ticketbooth, acquired by Leap Event Technology in 2019, has fully rebranded under the global Leap name as part of an APAC expansion, bundling ticketing, event apps, attendee engagement, convention management, marketing and analytics into one connected platform.

### Highlights
- Ticketbooth (Australia) rebrands fully as Leap Event Technology, five years after its 2019 acquisition.
- Leap now offers a combined suite: ticketing, event apps, attendee engagement, convention management, marketing, analytics.

### Industry Problem
Event organizers often juggle disparate point tools for ticketing, engagement, and analytics; consolidation under one brand/platform reflects demand for a single connected event-technology stack.

### Proposed Solution
Leap's response is a full-suite rebrand/consolidation; for Etix this is a competitive-structure signal to monitor — whether bundled all-in-one platforms are winning organizers away from best-of-breed ticketing on its own.

### Key Points
- Leap Event Technology completes rebrand of its Australian Ticketbooth acquisition, five years post-acquisition.
- New unified suite spans ticketing through post-event analytics.
- Positioned as an APAC growth move.

**Leap Event Technology has expanded its reach in the Asia-Pacific region following the rebrand of Australian business Ticketbooth.**

The business, which was first acquired by Leap Event Technology in 2019, will now operate fully under the global Leap brand.

Central to the rebrand is the launch of a connected suite of event technology designed to cover the full event lifecycle. Leap will now offer ticketing, event apps, attendee engagement, convention management, marketing and analytics together under one provider.

“Ticketbooth has already earned its reputation in Australia as a trusted ticketing partner, and becoming Leap allows us to take that to the next level,” said Leap Event Technology general manager for APAC, Jess Attwood.

“We’re helping organisers rethink the entire event experience – how they sell, how they engage audiences, how they understand attendees and how they create new revenue streams.

“It’s about combining our local knowledge and relationships with Leap’s global experience and technology, giving organisers a connected platform and team to help them grow and deliver better experiences.

“We are helping organisers rethink the entire event experience, how they sell, how they engage audiences, how they understand attendees and how they open new revenue streams.”

The company works with organisers across festivals, music, [conventions](https://www.theticketingbusiness.com/2025/01/leap-event-technology-debuts-solutions-in-australia/), agricultural shows, arts and culture, sports and exhibitions, including Supernova, St Kilda Festival, the Art Gallery of South Australia and Taste of Summer.
