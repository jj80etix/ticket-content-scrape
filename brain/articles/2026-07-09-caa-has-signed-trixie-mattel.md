Creative Artists Agency has signed singer and actor Trixie Mattel for representation in comedy and television, the company recently announced.

Trixie is a drag queen and recognized “Skinny Legend” who won season three of *RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars*. She built a successful touring career, performing sets all over the globe and co-headlining tours with fellow drag queen Katya. The comedic duo hosts The Bald and the Beautiful podcast, a successful venture that they took on the road this summer.

In addition to her tours and podcast, Trixie also released the documentary *Trixie Mattel: Moving Parts*, launched Trixie Cosmetics, premiered the *Skinny Legend* comedy special and co-authored *Trixie and Katya’s Guide to Modern Womanhood*.

Trixie is also performing her immersive, sci-fi-inspired Super Disco DJ set at venues and major festivals, including Lollapalooza, Osheaga, and Outside Lands.

One of Trixie’s most successful outings in 2025 was a sold-out show she headlined at The Salt Shed – Outdoor in Chicago, where she grossed $282,643 off 5,443 tickets, according to Pollstar Boxoffice.

The multihyphenate star is managed by Jacob Slane, David Charpentier, Ryan Aceto and Maddie Jacobs of Producer Entertainment Group.
