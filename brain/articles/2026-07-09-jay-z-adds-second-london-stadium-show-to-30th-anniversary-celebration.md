# Jay-Z Adds Second London Stadium Show to 30th Anniversary Celebration

## Summary

Jay-Z has added a second London Stadium performance (Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, September 4) to his 30th-anniversary tour, joining previously announced Paris and Los Angeles dates as well as hometown Yankee Stadium shows marking the anniversaries of Reasonable Doubt and The Blueprint.

### Key Points
- New Tottenham Hotspur Stadium date added September 4, alongside existing Paris (Sept 10) and LA SoFi Stadium (Oct 23) shows.
- Anniversary celebration also includes Yankee Stadium shows July 10-11 marking Reasonable Doubt (30th) and The Blueprint (25th).

## Transcript

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/Jay-Z-London.webp)

Jay-Z is expanding his 30th anniversary career celebration with an additional performance in London this fall.

The hip-hop artist has announced a new date at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on September 4. The newly announced London performance joins previously revealed anniversary shows in Paris and Los Angeles. Jay-Z is scheduled to perform in Paris on September 10 before heading to Los Angeles for a concert at SoFi Stadium on October 23.

The added London date comes as the rapper prepares for his hometown celebration in New York. Jay-Z will take the stage at Yankee Stadium on July 10 and 11 for a pair of performances commemorating significant milestones from his career, including the 30th anniversary of his debut album, *Reasonable Doubt*, and the 25th anniversary of *The Blueprint*.

Jay-Z began his summer anniversary celebrations with a headline performance at The Roots Picnic in Philadelphia on May 30.
