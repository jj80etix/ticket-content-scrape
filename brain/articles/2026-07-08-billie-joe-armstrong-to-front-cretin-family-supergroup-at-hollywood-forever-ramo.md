# Billie Joe Armstrong To Front Cretin Family Supergroup At Hollywood Forever Ramones 50th Anniversary Event

## Summary

Green Day frontman Billie Joe Armstrong will lead Cretin Family, a punk supergroup featuring Rancid's Tim Armstrong, blink-182's Travis Barker, and CJ Ramone, at a Ramones 50th Anniversary tribute event at Hollywood Forever Cemetery in Los Angeles on August 30. The event, hosted by John Travolta, pairs the band's first-ever live performance with a 50th-anniversary screening of "Carrie" and a preview of Travolta's new film, plus a DJ set from Shepard Fairey. Proceeds benefit the David Agus MD Cancer Research fund at the Ellison Institute.

### Key Points
- Cretin Family (Billie Joe Armstrong, Tim Armstrong, Travis Barker, CJ Ramone) debuts live at the event, performing Ramones songs with unannounced special guests.
- Event takes place August 30 at Hollywood Forever Cemetery, Los Angeles.
- Hosted by John Travolta, including a "Carrie" 50th-anniversary screening and a preview of his new film "Propeller One-Way Night Coach."
- Shepard Fairey will DJ the event.
- Proceeds support cancer research at the Ellison Institute.
- Tickets are on sale via TicketWeb.
