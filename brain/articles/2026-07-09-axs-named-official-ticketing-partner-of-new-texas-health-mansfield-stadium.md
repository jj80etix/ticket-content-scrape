# AXS Named Official Ticketing Partner of New Texas Health Mansfield Stadium

## Summary

AXS has been named the official ticketing partner of the new Texas Health Mansfield Stadium, extending the Tier 2 consumer-ticketing platform's footprint in a newly built venue market.

### Highlights
- AXS secures exclusive ticketing rights for the new Texas Health Mansfield Stadium.
- Adds to AXS's roster of venue-partnership wins in the competitive primary-ticketing market.

### Industry Problem
New/emerging venues represent a recurring competitive battleground where primary ticketing platforms compete on integration, fees, and service to win exclusive partner status — a channel independents like Etix must also contest.

### Proposed Solution
The article describes only the partnership win, not a specific product feature; Etix should track which venue-partnership deals AXS and other Tier 2 platforms are winning to benchmark competitive positioning and identify similar new-venue opportunities to pursue directly.

### Key Points
- AXS named official ticketing partner of Texas Health Mansfield Stadium, a newly built venue.
- Signals continued AXS expansion into new stadium/venue partnerships.

![](https://www.ticketnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/AXS-Texas-Health-Mansfield-Stadium.webp)

AXS has been selected as the Official Ticketing Partner of Texas Health Mansfield Stadium, the newly opened sports and entertainment venue in Mansfield, Texas.

The agreement comes after the stadium officially opened its doors on July 1 and marks another addition to AXS’ portfolio of sports and entertainment venues. Under the partnership, AXS will oversee ticketing operations for all events at Texas Health Mansfield Stadium.

Fans attending events at the venue will use the company’s ticketing platform, including its AXS Mobile ID technology, designed to provide secure mobile ticket access and management.

Texas Health Mansfield Stadium is positioned as a new destination for sports and live entertainment in Texas. The venue serves as the home stadium for North Texas SC, the MLS NEXT Pro affiliate of FC Dallas, and has already attracted international sporting events during its first weeks of operation.
